### Previous race

John Watson won the inaugural Detroit Grand Prix from 17th on the grid, working through the field to take the lead at lap 37 as Keke Rosberg lost third gear. Eddie Cheever claimed second for his best Grand Prix result. Watson takes the lead of the Drivers' Championship.

### Championship standings

Watson leads the championship going into Montreal. Pironi and Prost are close behind.

### Circuit

The race moves to June for the first time — the organisers have shifted the Canadian Grand Prix from autumn to take advantage of warmer weather. The circuit carries the name Circuit Gilles Villeneuve, honouring the Ferrari driver who died at Zolder six weeks ago.
