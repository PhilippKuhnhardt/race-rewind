### Championship standings

Hamilton leads the championship on 88 points after Canada. Alonso is second on 86, Vettel third on 85 — three drivers within three points of the lead after seven races.

### Previous race

Hamilton won in Montreal from Grosjean and Pérez. The win moved Hamilton to the championship lead for the first time since Australia; Alonso and Vettel slipped to second and third after failing to split the leading group.

### Entrants

Jules Bianchi drives for Force India in the first practice session, replacing Nico Hülkenberg. Valtteri Bottas again takes Bruno Senna's Williams seat in FP1.

### Tyre choices

Pirelli brings the white-banded medium compound as the prime and yellow-banded soft as the option — the same combination as last year's Valencia race. The FIA has implemented a single longer DRS zone for 2012, replacing the two zones used in 2011. The detection point is at the approach to Turn 8, with activation between Turns 10 and 11 — the activation point brought forward 70 metres compared to 2011.

### Weather

Fine and dry conditions are forecast for race day — 27°C air temperature, 45°C track.
