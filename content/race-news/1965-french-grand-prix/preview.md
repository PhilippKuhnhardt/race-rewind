### Championship standings

Clark leads with 18 points after three races, Hill 3 points behind on 15, and debutant Stewart a further 4 back on 11. In the Manufacturers' Championship, BRM leads on 19 points, Lotus second on 18, Ferrari third on 12.

### Previous race

At Spa in the rain, Clark took the lead through the Masta Kink — the most dangerous corner on the circuit — and pulled out a massive advantage, at one point lapping Hill. Stewart finished second, McLaren third, Brabham fourth, Hill fifth. Richard Attwood crashed at Masta, his Lotus breaking in half and catching fire; Attwood escaped with minor burns.

### Track changes

The French Grand Prix is held at the Circuit de Charade near Clermont-Ferrand for the first time, having previously been run at Rouen-Les-Essarts. The circuit has been described as a quicker, twistier version of the Nürburgring.
