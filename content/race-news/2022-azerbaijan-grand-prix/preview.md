### Championship standings

Max Verstappen leads the Drivers' Championship by nine points from Charles Leclerc in second and fifteen from Sergio Pérez in third after the Monaco Grand Prix. Red Bull Racing leads Ferrari by 36 points and Mercedes by 101 in the Constructors' Championship.

### Previous race

Pérez won Monaco after a strategy error from Ferrari dropped Leclerc from pole position to fourth. The race start was aborted — two formation laps had been run sixteen minutes after the scheduled start time — before restarting fifty minutes late. Mick Schumacher's car split in two at the swimming pool section, ending the race under red flag conditions that were restarted. Sainz finished second, Verstappen third.

### Tyre choices

### Practice
