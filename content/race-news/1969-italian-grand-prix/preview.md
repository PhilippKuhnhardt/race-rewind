### Championship standings

Stewart leads the championship by a commanding margin after six wins from seven races. Ickx won Germany to move into second in the standings, but the gap to the Scot remains large.

### Previous race

Ickx won the German Grand Prix at the Nürburgring. He made a poor start and dropped to ninth, then worked through the entire field over three laps to join Stewart at the front. The pair battled nose-to-tail for two full laps before Ickx passed under braking at the Südkehre; Stewart's gearbox then began to slow him and Ickx pulled clear to win by a minute. McLaren and Hill completed the podium. Only four Formula One cars finished the race.

### Championship permutations

If Stewart wins at Monza, the Drivers' Championship is decided this weekend.
