### Championship standings

Verstappen leads the championship with 44 points, a single point ahead of Pérez. Alonso is third, 14 points off the lead. Red Bull lead the Constructors' Championship with 87 points; Aston Martin and Mercedes are joint second with 38 points each.

### Previous race

At Jeddah, Pérez took pole and won, with Verstappen recovering from a driveshaft failure in qualifying that left him 15th on the grid to finish second. Alonso claimed his 100th Formula One podium in third. The result — and a post-race investigation that briefly dropped Alonso to fourth before his position was reinstated — left Verstappen and Pérez separated by just one point.

### Tyre choices

Pirelli brings the C2, C3, and C4 compounds — designated hard, medium, and soft.

### Track changes

Following the 2021 Albert Park redevelopment and positive FIA and Formula One feedback, the fourth DRS zone between turns 8 and 9 — removed from the 2022 race after safety concerns from drivers during practice — is reinstated for this edition.
