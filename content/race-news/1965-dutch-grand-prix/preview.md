### Championship standings

Clark has won four of five races. Hill and Stewart remain the closest challengers, though the gap is widening.

### Previous race

At Silverstone, Clark led away from pole with Hill close behind. With 16 laps remaining, Clark's oil pressure began dropping every lap; he responded by killing the engine through the fast corners, costing at least 2 seconds a lap. Hill, who had earlier suffered brake trouble, closed rapidly but could not find a way past — Clark crossed the line just 3 seconds ahead. Hill second, Surtees third.

### Between-race developments

The Dutch Grand Prix has moved from its traditional mid-May slot to mid-July this season, placing it just a week after the British Grand Prix.

Honda conducted extensive testing at Zandvoort ahead of this round.
