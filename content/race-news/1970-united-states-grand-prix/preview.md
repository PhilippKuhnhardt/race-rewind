### Previous race

Ickx won in Canada from second on the grid, with Regazzoni second and Amon third in another Ferrari-dominated afternoon. Stewart led comfortably in the Tyrrell 001 for 31 laps before left front suspension failure ended his run.

### Championship permutations

Ickx must win this race and the Mexican Grand Prix to stand a chance of overtaking Rindt's posthumous points total.

### Entrants

Reine Wisell makes his Formula One debut alongside Fittipaldi at Lotus. Graham Hill, running privately in a Lotus 72 for Rob Walker, arrives with his overalls soaked in fuel from a fitting that came loose during practice and is being repaired by the team.

### Qualifying

Ickx is on pole for Ferrari. The final qualifying session was disrupted by a downpour that left only 15 minutes of dry running, denying Stewart the chance to improve; he starts second. Fittipaldi, who spent the first half of the season in European Formula Two, is just five hundredths behind Stewart in third. A crowd exceeding 100,000 has attended Watkins Glen for the second straight year.
