### Previous race

At Nivelles, Fittipaldi won for McLaren after Regazzoni ran onto the grass avoiding a backmarker, handing the lead away. Lauda finished second, Scheckter third after Regazzoni ran out of fuel pump pressure on the final lap while trying to reclaim the podium.
