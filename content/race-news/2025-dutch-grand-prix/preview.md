### Championship standings

Piastri leads the Drivers' Championship with 284 points, nine ahead of Norris and ninety-seven ahead of Verstappen in third.

### Previous race

Norris won at the Hungaroring for McLaren's 200th Formula One victory, making them the second team after Ferrari to reach that total. Piastri challenged Norris on the penultimate lap, locking up and coming within centimetres of his teammate's rear wing, but could not pass. Russell finished third. McLaren's fourth consecutive one-two finish was their longest run since Mercedes completed five in a row at the start of 2019.

### Circuit change

The pit lane speed limit at Zandvoort has been raised from 60 km/h to 80 km/h.

### Milestones

This is Pirelli's 500th Formula One Grand Prix as tyre supplier.

### Tyre choices

Pirelli brings the C2, C3 and C4 compounds — designated hard, medium and soft.

### Practice

Norris topped all three practice sessions. FP1 — Norris, Piastri, Stroll — was red-flagged when Antonelli beached his Mercedes at turn 9. FP2 — Norris, Alonso, Piastri — saw two red flags: Stroll crashed heavily at turn 3, and Albon beached his Williams at turn 1. FP3 closed with Norris, Piastri and Russell at the top.
