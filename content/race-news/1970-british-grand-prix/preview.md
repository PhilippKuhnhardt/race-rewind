### Championship standings

Rindt has taken the championship lead after winning in France, moving ahead of Stewart.

### Previous race

Rindt won at Clermont-Ferrand from sixth on the grid, fighting through the field despite a stomach ulcer and a facial cut from a practice-session stone. Ickx's engine misfired into retirement on lap 16; Stewart pitted with engine issues. Beltoise, threatening to win in France for France, suffered a slow rear puncture and had to settle for less.

### Entrants

Emerson Fittipaldi makes his Formula One debut for Lotus, qualifying 21st in the older Lotus 49. The Surtees constructor also makes its World Championship debut this weekend.

### Qualifying

Rindt is on pole at Brands Hatch, with Brabham having equalled Rindt's time on Goodyear tyres. Ickx completes the front row. Stewart starts only eighth — the March does not suit the bumpy, off-camber Brands Hatch layout. Rindt's Firestone tyres are not working perfectly in the high summer temperatures.
