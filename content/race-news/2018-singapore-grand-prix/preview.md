### Championship standings

Lewis Hamilton leads Sebastian Vettel by thirty points in the Drivers' Championship. Mercedes lead Ferrari by twenty-five points in the Constructors'.

### Previous race

Hamilton won at Monza, overtaking Räikkönen on lap 45 to take his sixth win of the season. Räikkönen finished second, Bottas third after a collision with Verstappen. Sergey Sirotkin scored his first championship point, finishing tenth after Grosjean's disqualification — meaning every driver who entered the 2018 championship has now scored at least one point, the first time this has happened in the series' history.
