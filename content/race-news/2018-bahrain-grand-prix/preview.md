### Championship standings

Sebastian Vettel leads Lewis Hamilton by seven points in the Drivers' Championship. Ferrari hold a ten-point lead over Mercedes in the Constructors'.

### Previous race

Vettel won the season opener in Melbourne, using a timely virtual safety car period — triggered by Grosjean's stricken Haas — to pass Hamilton and then hold the position to the finish. Räikkönen finished third, Ricciardo fourth.

### Practice

Ricciardo was fastest in FP1. Räikkönen led FP2 and FP3.
