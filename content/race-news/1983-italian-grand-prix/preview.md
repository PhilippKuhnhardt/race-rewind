### Championship standings

Prost leads with Arnoux eight points behind in second. Tambay and Piquet are joint third, 14 points behind the leader. Ferrari leads the Constructors' Championship by 12 points from Renault, with three races remaining.

### Previous race

Arnoux won the Dutch Grand Prix after Prost and Piquet collided on lap 41 while fighting for the lead, eliminating both. Tambay inherited second in what became a Ferrari 1–2 until Patrese, running close behind, suffered a problem near the end and dropped back. Watson finished third in the Cosworth-powered McLaren. Lauda debuted the TAG Porsche turbo but retired with brake failure on lap 26.

### Between-race developments

John Watson also moves to the TAG Porsche turbo engine for Monza, making this McLaren's first race with both cars on turbo power.
