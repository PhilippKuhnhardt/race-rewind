### Championship standings

Prost leads the championship after his win at Imola.

### Previous race

Prost won at Imola in a McLaren 1–2 that was less comfortable than it appeared — a carbon brake overheating problem mid-race caused him to spin through the infield, necessitating a tyre stop. Arnoux was second, de Angelis third despite running out of fuel on the last lap.

### Track changes

This is the final Formula One race to be held at the 3.801 km Dijon-Prenois circuit. FISA has deemed the venue too short — during the 1984 season, cars are lapping in the 1m 2s range, and the governing body has stipulated that any lap under a minute does not count as a completed lap.
