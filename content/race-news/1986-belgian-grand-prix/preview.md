### Previous race

At Monaco, Prost won for the third consecutive year, with Rosberg second and Senna third. It was Elio de Angelis's final race.

### Between-race developments

Three days after Monaco, de Angelis was killed in a testing accident at Circuit Paul Ricard. His car caught fire after a rear wing failure and he could not be extracted quickly enough; he died of his injuries the following day. He was 28. Brabham enter only one car at Spa — Riccardo Patrese's BT55 — with no replacement named yet.

### Circuit

The Belgian Grand Prix has been moved to May this year, earlier than its usual summer slot. The change follows the 1985 race, when the tarmac at Spa-Francorchamps broke up in practice under the power of the turbo cars; organisers want to avoid the dry heat that accelerated the damage.
