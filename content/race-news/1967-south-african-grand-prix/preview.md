### Entrants

The Kyalami circuit near Johannesburg is hosting a World Championship Formula One race for the first time. Several driver changes take effect this weekend: John Surtees makes his Honda debut, Graham Hill races for the first time in Lotus colours, and Mike Spence steps into Hill's old BRM seat. Pedro Rodríguez is on trial for Cooper.
