### Championship standings

Hamilton leads the Drivers' Championship by 12 points from Alonso, with Räikkönen 17 points behind Hamilton. Hamilton needs to finish within one point of Alonso and within six points of Räikkönen to clinch the title this weekend. Whatever the outcome, Hamilton will still lead the championship after this race.

### Previous race

Hamilton won in Japan at Fuji in heavy rain and misty conditions. Kovalainen finished second for Renault — the team's only podium of the season. Räikkönen was third. Alonso crashed out on lap 41 of 67, allowing Hamilton to extend his lead to 12 points.

### Weather

The track weather is affected by Typhoon Krosa, which is hitting the east coast of China.
