### Championship standings

Fangio leads the World Championship after winning the season opener in Switzerland.

### Previous race

At Bremgarten, Fangio won from pole despite pitting mid-race. Taruffi finished second in just his second championship appearance; Farina third after his strategy of running without a pit stop did not pay off. Ascari drove to sixth despite burns from a Formula 2 accident at Genoa the weekend before. Stirling Moss, on his World Championship debut with HWM, ran out of fuel on the final lap.

### Entrants

The Indianapolis 500 is held on a Wednesday, three days after the Swiss Grand Prix. For the second consecutive year, no European Formula One teams have entered. The race is run under AAA regulations and is not held to FIA Grand Prix rules.

Duke Nalon takes pole position in a Novi — his comeback after suffering serious burns in a crash at Indianapolis in 1949, which caused him to miss the 1950 event. Heavy attrition is expected on a field dominated by Kurtis Kraft-Offenhauser machinery.
