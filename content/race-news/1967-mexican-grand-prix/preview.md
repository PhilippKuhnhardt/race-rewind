### Championship standings

Hulme leads on 47 points; Brabham is five behind on 42. For the first time in eight years, two teammates enter the final race both able to win the championship.

### Championship permutations

Hulme needs a fourth-place finish or better to claim the title. Brabham must win the race and have Hulme finish fifth or lower.

### Previous race

Clark won at Watkins Glen despite his right-rear suspension breaking two laps from the end, nursing the damaged car to the flag six seconds ahead of Hill. Hulme finished third, a lap down, with his engine sputtering on fuel. Amon ran a strong third until his engine ran out of oil with twelve laps remaining.
