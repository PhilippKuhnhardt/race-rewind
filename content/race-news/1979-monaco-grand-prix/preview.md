### Previous race

Scheckter won in Belgium with Laffite second and Pironi third — Pironi's first Formula One podium. Villeneuve was seventh after running out of fuel on the final lap.

### Championship standings

Scheckter's Belgium win moved him to the top of the standings with 25 points, one ahead of Laffite. Reutemann is third with 21, and Villeneuve and Depailler each have 20. The title is wide open.

### Entrants

Rebaque has not appeared and Alfa Romeo are staying at home. McLaren has a new version of the M28 for Watson; Tambay fails to make the cut.
