### Championship standings

Lauda extended his lead to 29 points in Belgium, where Ferrari dominated from the front row. Hunt retired with a transmission failure. The Spanish GP appeal remains unresolved.

### Previous race

Ferrari locked out the front row at Zolder and Lauda was unchallenged from the start. Regazzoni completed a Ferrari 1–2, with Laffite inheriting third after Hunt's transmission failed. A Ford-powered car's 67-consecutive-podium streak came to an end. Alan Jones took fifth for Surtees.

### Entrants

The Belgian and Monaco Grands Prix have swapped places on the calendar this year — Belgium ran two weeks ago, Monaco follows now. The grid is limited to 20 cars.
