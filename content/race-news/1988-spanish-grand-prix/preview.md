### Championship standings

Prost leads Senna by five points — 84 to 79 — entering Jerez. Two races remain.

### Between-race developments

Benetton has confirmed that British Formula 3000 driver Johnny Herbert will join the team for the 1989 season, replacing Thierry Boutsen who moves to Williams. Herbert is still recovering from a serious crash at Brands Hatch in the F3000 championship six weeks ago. Alessandro Nannini remains at Benetton as lead driver, with the team holding an option on him through 1990.
