### Championship standings

Hulme leads the championship; Brabham closed to within nine points with his Canadian win, re-opening the title fight between the two Brabham teammates.

### Previous race

Brabham won in Canada by over a minute after Clark's Lotus cut out in the rain when he was closing on the lead, and Hulme had to pit for clean goggles. Gurney finished third in the Eagle after running second for much of the race.
