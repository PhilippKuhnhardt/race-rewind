### Championship standings

Fangio has won the first three Formula One rounds of the season — Argentina, Monaco, and France. Musso and Collins provide Ferrari's best challenge in the standings, but the gap to Fangio remains substantial.

### Previous race

At Rouen, Fangio won from Musso and Collins, giving Lancia-Ferrari a 2–3–4 finish behind the Maserati. Behra led early but slipped behind Hawthorn in the closing stages. Ron Flockhart's BRM was seriously damaged in a high-speed accident during practice, though he was uninjured. Herbert MacKay-Fraser, making his Formula One debut, ran as high as sixth before a transmission failure ended his race at one-third distance.

### Between-race developments

MacKay-Fraser was killed days after the French Grand Prix in a Formula 2 race at the Reims-Gueux circuit. He made only the one championship start.

### Circuit

The British Grand Prix moves to Aintree this year, set within the horse-racing course that hosts the Grand National, rather than Silverstone. The two circuits share the hosting of the race on an alternating basis.

### Milestones

This is Maserati's 50th start in World Championship Formula One racing.
