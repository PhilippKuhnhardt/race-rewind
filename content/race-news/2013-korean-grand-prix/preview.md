### Championship standings

Sebastian Vettel leads the Drivers' Championship by 60 points over Fernando Alonso after Singapore, with Lewis Hamilton a further 36 points back and Kimi Räikkönen two points behind Hamilton.

### Previous race

Sebastian Vettel dominated Singapore for his third consecutive victory, leading every lap from pole, setting the fastest lap, and winning by more than 32 seconds — his third grand slam of the season. Alonso climbed from seventh on the grid to second, with Räikkönen third despite starting from 13th. Nico Rosberg finished fourth after a tyre strategy that did not work as planned.

### Between-race developments

Mark Webber has been issued his third reprimand of the season, for hitching a ride back to the pits on Fernando Alonso's Ferrari after retiring at Singapore. Under FIA regulations, a driver's third reprimand in a season carries an automatic ten-place grid penalty; Webber starts from the back of the top ten.
