### Previous race

Hill and Schumacher collided at Silverstone while fighting for the lead and both retired, promoting their teammates to the front. Herbert took victory — his first in Formula One — ahead of Alesi, with Coulthard third after a stop-go penalty for pit lane speeding. Panis, Blundell, and Frentzen completed the points. The collision brought their season-long tension to a head.

### Milestones

Schumacher races at his home circuit, the Hockenheimring. Hill holds pole position heading into the race.
