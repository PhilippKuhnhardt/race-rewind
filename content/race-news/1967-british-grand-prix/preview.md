### Championship standings

Hulme continues to lead the championship. Brabham closed the gap at Le Mans with his first win of the season.

### Previous race

Brabham won at Le Mans by 49.5 seconds from teammate Hulme after both Lotuses retired before half-distance — Clark from the lead on lap 23, Hill from the lead on lap 14, both with crown-wheel and pinion failures. Gurney also retired with a fuel line break. Jackie Stewart brought his BRM home third, a lap down, as only six cars finished again.

### Circuit

The British Grand Prix returns to Silverstone this year, continuing the event-sharing arrangement with Brands Hatch that has been in place since 1964.
