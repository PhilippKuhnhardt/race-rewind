### Championship standings

Jean Behra leads the Drivers' Championship after three rounds, one point ahead of Fangio and two ahead of Moss. Indianapolis 500 winner Pat Flaherty holds 8 points — level with Moss — despite having no Grand Prix entries.

### Previous race

The previous championship round was the Indianapolis 500 on 30 May, just four days ago. None of the regular Grand Prix field competed; Pat Flaherty won to earn 8 World Championship points. The last European championship race was Monaco on 13 May, where Moss won and Fangio took second in a shared drive with Collins.

### Entrants

Paco Godia joins the Maserati team for his first championship appearance of the season.

### Practice

On the first day of practice, Fangio sets a time almost five seconds faster than second-placed Moss. Wet conditions on Friday and strong winds on Saturday prevent any improvement, leaving the Thursday times as the grid order.
