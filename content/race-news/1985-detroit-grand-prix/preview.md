### Championship standings

Michele Alboreto leads the Drivers' Championship by five points from Alain Prost and Elio de Angelis after Canada. Ferrari also lead the Constructors' Championship.

### Previous race

At Montreal, Alboreto won for Ferrari ahead of teammate Stefan Johansson — Ferrari's first 1–2 since the 1983 Dutch Grand Prix. Prost finished third. De Angelis, who started from pole, came home fifth.

### Entrants

This is the only United States Grand Prix of 1985 — the Dallas race was cancelled before the season, leaving Detroit as the sole American round for the first time in ten years. The inside wall at the left-right sequence on Beaubien Street has been moved closer to the street, making the complex slower than before.
