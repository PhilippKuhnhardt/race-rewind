### Previous race

Schumacher won at Spa despite qualifying 16th, in an intense battle with Hill that saw the lead change hands multiple times as the track alternated between wet and dry. Hill finished second after being served a 10-second stop-go penalty for pit lane speeding. Brundle took a surprise third for Ligier in his last podium finish. After the race, Schumacher received a one-race suspended ban for blocking Hill too aggressively on the circuit's high-speed sections.

### Penalties

Schumacher carries a one-race suspended ban from Belgium for aggressive driving. It is suspended rather than immediately applied, but any further infringement could activate it.
