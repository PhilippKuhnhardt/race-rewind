### Previous race

At Imola, Senna won from pole ahead of Berger — McLaren's first 1-2 since the 1989 Belgian Grand Prix. JJ Lehto claimed the only podium finish in Dallara's history. Both Lotus drivers, Häkkinen and Bailey, scored championship points for the first time; an unexpected result for a team that had barely got their cars onto the grid.
