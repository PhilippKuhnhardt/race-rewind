### Championship standings

Farina leads the title fight heading into Reims, with Fagioli now just four points adrift after his second-place finish in Belgium. Fangio is a further point behind Fagioli.

### Previous race

At Spa, Fangio won to close on Farina, with Fagioli second for the fourth time in five starts. Rosier again made the podium in his Talbot-Lago. Farina suffered transmission trouble in the closing laps and dropped to fourth, though he salvaged the point for fastest lap.

### Entrants

Scuderia Ferrari have withdrawn both works entries for Villoresi and Ascari, who left the race during practice. The Talbot-Lago team arrives with dual-ignition engines fitted with twelve spark plugs, hoping the high-speed Reims-Gueux public road circuit — made up almost entirely of long straights — will suit their cars. Eugène Chaboud did not start in his own entered Talbot; instead he will co-drive with Philippe Étancelin. Franco Comotti has not attended.

Peter Whitehead, running a privateer Ferrari, is in the field for his first start of the season.

### Background

Fangio set a practice lap at 187 km/h (116 mph) — a striking marker of pace on this fast circuit.
