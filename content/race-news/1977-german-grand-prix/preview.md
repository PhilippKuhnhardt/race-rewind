### Previous race

At Silverstone, Watson led from the start after Hunt made a poor getaway, but Watson's fuel system failed 15 laps from the end. Hunt inherited the lead and took his first win of the season, the crowd's relief at a British champion winning his home race considerable. Lauda was second and Nilsson third. Villeneuve debuted, qualifying ninth. Jabouille's Renault turbo ran 16 laps before the turbocharger failed.

### Track changes

The German Grand Prix moves to Hockenheimring. The Nürburgring is not used due to concerns over the ability of organizers to marshal the vast circuit safely — concerns sharpened by Lauda's near-fatal accident there in 1976. This is only the second time the German Grand Prix is held at Hockenheim, the first being in 1970.

### Milestones

Scheckter takes the first pole position of his season. The race start is given by the drop of the German national flag after the standard red/green light system was damaged by a service vehicle before the race.
