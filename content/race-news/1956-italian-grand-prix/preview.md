### Championship standings

Fangio leads the championship with 30 points, eight clear of both Collins and Behra, who are level on 22.

### Previous race

Fangio won comfortably at the Nürburgring ahead of Moss and Behra. Behra's fifth podium of the season lifts him level with Collins in second place.

### Championship permutations

Collins can only win the championship by winning the Italian Grand Prix while Fangio scores three points or fewer — a result that, under the "best five scores" rule, would not count for Fangio — leaving both men on 30 points with Collins winning on countback. The fastest lap point could prove decisive.

### Entrants

Wolfgang von Trips makes his championship debut with Scuderia Ferrari. He crashed in practice at around 130 mph, was thrown from the car, and escaped with scratches and bruises; the car was a complete wreck. The Ferrari team attributed it to the risks of a young driver in a fast car and did not recognise it had been caused by a tyre blowout.

Jo Bonnier makes his championship debut — the first Swedish driver to do so. Les Leston also makes his first World Championship start.

### Practice

Fangio takes his sixth pole position of the season, ahead of teammates Castellotti and Musso.
