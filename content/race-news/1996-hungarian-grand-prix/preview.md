### Championship standings

Hill leads Villeneuve by 21 points with five races remaining after extending his advantage at Hockenheim.

### Previous race

At Hockenheim, Hill won from pole for his seventh victory of the season. Berger started alongside Hill on the front row and led for much of the race, pulling away clearly — until his engine failed three laps from the end. Alesi inherited second, Villeneuve third. Hill's win extended his lead over Villeneuve to 21 points.
