### Championship standings

Clark is champion. Ginther, Hill, and Surtees are the three drivers still contesting second place in the standings.

### Previous race

At Watkins Glen, Clark's battery was found dead on the dummy grid as the field moved to their starting positions. By the time the Lotus crew replaced it, the last-placed Stebro had already completed its second lap. Clark rejoined over a lap down. Hill led from pole, surrendered the front to Surtees, then battled to retake it before losing ground with handling problems after his anti-roll bar came loose. Surtees led until his engine lost power with 28 laps remaining, handing the win to Hill.

### Entrants

This is the inaugural Mexican Grand Prix, the first Formula One World Championship race held in Mexico. The circuit is the Ciudad Deportiva Magdalena Mixhuca in Mexico City. Moisés Solana and Frank Dochnal make their Formula One World Championship debuts.

This is the only World Championship Grand Prix in which a car will race bearing the number 13.
