### Championship standings

Senna leads, but Mansell has momentum after winning France and arrives here on home ground with the crowd behind him.

### Previous race

At Magny-Cours, Mansell took the lead from Prost with an outbraking move around the outside at the Adelaide hairpin — the same move he had used to get past Prost earlier in the race — and pulled away to win his first race of the season. It was Mansell's 17th Formula 1 victory, surpassing Stirling Moss's record for the most wins by a British driver.

### Between-race developments

Tom Walkinshaw has purchased a 35% stake in the Benetton team. Stefan Johansson makes his final appearance standing in for the still-injured Alex Caffi at Footwork.

### Track changes

Silverstone has been extensively remodelled for 1991 and is no longer the simple, ultra-fast circuit it has been since its inception. The revised layout adds technical sections while remaining relatively quick, and has earned largely positive reviews from drivers.
