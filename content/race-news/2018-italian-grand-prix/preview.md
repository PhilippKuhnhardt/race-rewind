### Championship standings

Lewis Hamilton leads Sebastian Vettel by seventeen points in the Drivers' Championship. Mercedes lead Ferrari by fifteen points in the Constructors'.

### Previous race

Vettel won at Spa, passing Hamilton on the Kemmel Straight on the first lap and holding the position to the finish. The race opened with a multi-car incident at turn 1: Hülkenberg misjudged his braking, rammed into the back of Alonso, and flew over Leclerc's car, landing on the halo. Räikkönen and Ricciardo collided separately, ending both drivers' races. The newly formed Racing Point Force India qualified third and fourth, finishing fifth and sixth.
