### Previous race

At Monza, Lauda led a Ferrari 1–2 with Regazzoni until Lauda retired with a water leak, handing the lead to Regazzoni. Regazzoni's engine then failed ten laps from the end. Peterson inherited the lead and won, holding off Fittipaldi. Scheckter finished third to close up the championship with two races remaining.

### Entrants

Two American teams make their Formula One World Championship debuts this weekend. Penske enters with Mark Donohue driving their own chassis. Parnelli Jones enters a car inspired by the Lotus 72, with Mario Andretti behind the wheel.

### Qualifying

Fittipaldi takes pole position, just ahead of Lauda. Scheckter qualifies third.
