### Championship standings

Clark leads the championship with 18 points after winning Belgium and the Netherlands. Ginther is second on 11, McLaren and Gurney are joint third on 10. Hill has fallen behind after failing to score in Belgium.

### Previous race

At Zandvoort, Clark started from pole and led a race of attrition. Brabham ran second early before falling back; Gurney staged a recovery from a bad start, climbing to third. Hill's BRM overheated and he was eventually forced to pit on lap 58, surrendering his position to Surtees. Clark finished a full lap clear of the rest.

### Entrants

The French Grand Prix returns to Reims-Gueux, having alternated between Reims and Rouen-Les-Essarts in recent years. Peter Arundell makes his Formula One World Championship debut.

### Milestones

This is the 100th Formula One World Championship race in which a British driver has participated. Of those 100 races, British drivers have won 39, claimed 95 podiums, 38 pole positions, 43 fastest laps, and three World Championships.
