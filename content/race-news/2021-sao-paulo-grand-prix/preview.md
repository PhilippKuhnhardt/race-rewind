### Previous race

At Mexico City, Bottas was spun at the first corner by Ricciardo. A separate incident behind involving Tsunoda and Schumacher brought out the safety car. Verstappen took his second win in a row, ahead of Hamilton and Pérez in Pérez's home race.

### Sprint format

This is the third and final sprint qualifying event of the 2021 season. As at the British and Italian Grands Prix, Friday features qualifying to set the sprint grid, Saturday has the sprint race, and Sunday the main Grand Prix. The São Paulo Grand Prix returns to the calendar this year after the 2020 edition was cancelled due to the COVID-19 pandemic; it is renamed from the Brazilian Grand Prix to reflect increased involvement from the local government.
