### Previous race

Patrick Tambay won the German Grand Prix for Ferrari — his first Formula One victory — after leading from the early laps. Nelson Piquet had been running clear when he collided with Eliseo Salazar while lapping him at the new Ostkurve chicane; an enraged Piquet climbed from the stopped car and punched and kicked Salazar before marshals intervened. René Arnoux finished second and Rosberg third.

### Between-race developments

Championship leader Didier Pironi was seriously injured in qualifying at Hockenheim when his Ferrari struck the back of Alain Prost's slow-moving Renault at high speed in wet conditions, was thrown into the air, and cartwheeled to a stop. Pironi suffered severe leg injuries and will not race again this season. His championship lead is now in the hands of drivers who carry on.

### Championship standings

With Pironi absent, the championship is wide open. Rosberg moves into contention, and Watson is still within range. Several drivers remain mathematically in the fight.

### Track changes

A chicane has been added at the entrance to the pit lane at the Österreichring earlier this year.

### Strategy

Brabham plan to make mid-race fuel and tyre stops here for the first time — an approach they have been developing all season but have not yet successfully completed.
