### Previous race

Stewart won the South African Grand Prix from fourth on the grid, breaking the lap record while still heavy on fuel. Brabham's rear wing collapsed on lap 5 and was removed in the pits, his handling compromised enough to force retirement. Andretti inherited second before a gearbox failure; Rindt dropped out with a failing fuel pump. Hill and Hulme completed the podium.

### Entrants

The entry list is thin: the Targa Florio sportscar race falls on the same day, drawing teams away. Only twelve drivers manage to start.

### Circuit

This is the first Formula One World Championship race at the Montjuïc street circuit in Barcelona, though the circuit has existed since 1933.
