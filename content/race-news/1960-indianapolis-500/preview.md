### Context

The Indianapolis 500 appears on the Formula One World Championship calendar for the final time. The race runs under USAC regulations, not FIA rules, and its results are excluded from the Constructors' Championship. The Formula One drivers who typically compete on the European calendar do not take part; the field is composed of American oval specialists.

The race is scheduled for 30 May — the day after the Monaco Grand Prix.
