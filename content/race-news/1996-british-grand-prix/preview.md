### Championship standings

Hill leads after winning France for his sixth victory of the season. Ferrari have gone two consecutive races without either car finishing.

### Previous race

At Magny-Cours, Hill won from start to finish — he led every lap except for the pit stop periods. Villeneuve finished second, Alesi third for Benetton. Schumacher qualified on pole but his engine blew on the warm-up lap and he did not start the race. Irvine had his times deleted for a bodywork infringement and started from the back of the grid. Both Forti cars started but retired; it was the last Grand Prix in which a Forti entered car started a race.
