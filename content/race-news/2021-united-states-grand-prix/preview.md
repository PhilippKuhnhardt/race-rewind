### Championship standings

Verstappen leads Hamilton by six points — 262.5 to 256.5. Bottas is third with 177; Norris trails him by 32 points, and Pérez is ten points behind Norris. Mercedes lead the Constructors' by 36 points over Red Bull. McLaren are third with 240 points, 7.5 ahead of Ferrari; Alpine are fifth with 104.

### Previous race

At Istanbul, Hamilton was dropped down the grid due to a power unit component penalty. Bottas took pole and won the race — his first victory of 2021 — ahead of Verstappen, who re-took the championship lead, and Pérez.

### Tyre choices

### Practice

Bottas led FP1 ahead of Hamilton and Verstappen. Pérez topped FP2 ahead of Norris and Hamilton. In FP3, high winds and heat caught several drivers out; Verstappen and Hamilton both had lap times deleted for track limits violations. Pérez was again the fastest, ahead of Sainz and Verstappen.
