### Championship standings

Reutemann leads the championship after France. Piquet remains his main challenger.

### Previous race

At Dijon-Prenois, Piquet led until a torrential downpour on lap 58 halted the race. In the restart — with grid positions based on the finish order of the first segment — Prost took over and held the lead to the end. It was Prost's first Formula One victory, on home soil in a Renault. Watson was second; Piquet third in the aggregate classification despite finishing fifth on the road in the restart.

### Entrants

Team Lotus brings the new Lotus 88B to Silverstone, but FISA bans it once again. Having cannibalised the Lotus 87s to build the 88Bs, the team has no choice but to withdraw entirely.

### Milestones

This is McLaren's 200th Formula One World Championship start.

### Practice

Nelson Piquet tests the new Brabham BT50 with BMW turbocharged engine during practice sessions, but sets his qualifying time in the older BT49C.

### Circuit notes

The race takes place on a Saturday, rather than the customary Sunday.
