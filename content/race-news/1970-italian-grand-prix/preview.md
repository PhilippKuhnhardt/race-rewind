### Championship standings

Rindt leads with 45 points, ahead of Brabham on 25 and Hulme on 20. Ickx is pushing hard for Ferrari.

### Previous race

Ferrari dominated at the Österreichring. Ickx won with Regazzoni second after a tactical team play waved Ickx through — the pair finished 1-2 ahead of the whole field except Rolf Stommelen in third. Rindt retired on lap 21 with an engine failure, his first non-finish since Monaco.

### Practice

Teams have stripped their cars of aerodynamic devices for Monza's long straights, where speeds of over 300 km/h are possible — though the trade-off is instability through the corners. Emerson Fittipaldi crashed into the barriers at the Parabolica during practice and escaped unhurt, but left his Lotus mechanics with a significant repair.

Championship leader Jochen Rindt was killed on Saturday when his Lotus 72 left the track at the same Parabolica corner. He had been wearing only a lap belt and slid underneath it on impact. Team Lotus has withdrawn from the race.

### Qualifying

Ickx is on pole for Ferrari, ahead of Pedro Rodríguez (BRM), Clay Regazzoni (Ferrari), and Jackie Stewart (Tyrrell-March). Rindt had qualified 12th but will not start.
