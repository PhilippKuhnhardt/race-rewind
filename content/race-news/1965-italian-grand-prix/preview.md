### Championship standings

Clark secured the 1965 Drivers' Championship at the German Grand Prix. He enters Monza as confirmed World Champion with three rounds still to run — the earliest the championship had been decided up to that point. Hill and Stewart continue to race for second place in the standings.

### Previous race

At the Nürburgring, Clark led from pole to flag, set fastest lap, and won his fifth consecutive race — the first driver to achieve that feat since Jack Brabham in 1960. Hill was second, Gurney third. Surtees fell back at the start with gearbox trouble and retired on lap 11; Stewart's suspension failed and he also dropped out.
