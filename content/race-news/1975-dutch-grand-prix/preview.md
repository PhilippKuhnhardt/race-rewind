### Championship standings

Lauda leads by ten points after three consecutive wins at Monaco, Belgium, and Sweden. Reutemann has moved past Fittipaldi into second place.

### Previous race

At Anderstorp, Reutemann took the lead from Brambilla mid-race before Lauda worked his way through the field on a harder tyre compound, setting a series of fastest laps to overhaul Reutemann with ten laps remaining. Regazzoni and Brambilla completed the Ferrari-dominated top three. Brise earned Graham Hill's team their first constructor's point before his gearbox jammed.

### Practice

Zandvoort does not hold a Sunday morning warm-up session — the only round on the calendar without one. During Friday practice, Jean-Pierre Jarier crashes; the Maki team's weekend ends abruptly when their engine goes up in smoke. James Hunt's extra laps on Saturday afternoon — sent out after rain meant the session times could not improve — reveal a broken component in his metering unit. Without those unscheduled laps, the failure would not have been found before the race.
