### Championship standings

Depailler leads the championship for the first time after his Monaco victory. Reutemann and Andretti follow, with Lauda fourth and Peterson fifth.

### Previous race

Depailler took his maiden Formula One victory at Monaco, passing Watson early and pulling clear for a wire-to-wire win. Lauda recovered from a puncture to reclaim second, Scheckter third. Reutemann collided with Hunt on the opening lap and was forced to pit for repairs, effectively ending his race. Jean-Pierre Jabouille brought the Renault home tenth — the team's first race finish.

### Between-race developments

The new Lotus 79 is ready to race for the first time. Andretti will drive it at Zolder; Peterson continues in the older Lotus 78. The 79 has been developed specifically around Colin Chapman's ground-effect principles, using venturi-shaped underfloor tunnels with sliding skirts to generate downforce.
