### Championship standings

Alonso leads Räikkönen by 26 points and Schumacher by around 40 after his France and Britain victories.

### Previous race

Montoya won Silverstone by 2.7 seconds from Alonso, with Räikkönen third. Räikkönen had qualified second but received a second consecutive ten-place engine penalty, dropping him towards the back of the grid before charging through the field. Fisichella stalled in the pits on lap 46 while on course for a podium. It was Montoya's first win for McLaren.

### Between-race developments

Patrick Friesacher's Minardi race seat passes to Robert Doornbos. Doornbos had been Jordan's third driver for the first eleven rounds; Nicolas Kiesa takes over as Jordan's third driver from this race onwards.

### Milestones

This race marks Räikkönen's fifth consecutive race at Hockenheim without a finish.
