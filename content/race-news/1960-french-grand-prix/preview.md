### Previous race

Brabham won at Spa ahead of McLaren and Graham Hill. The Belgian Grand Prix weekend was marred by accidents in practice and fatal incidents during the race. Moss and Taylor were both injured before the start and did not compete.

### Between-race developments

Ron Flockhart joins Lotus, replacing the late Alan Stacey. Henry Taylor and Bruce Halford join the British Racing Partnership. Tony Brooks tests the new Vanwall VW11 here at Reims before returning to his BRP Cooper drive.

Stirling Moss remains absent, still recovering from the broken legs sustained at Spa.

### Entrants

John Surtees makes his Formula One World Championship debut with Lotus, transferring from an extraordinary career in motorcycle racing.
