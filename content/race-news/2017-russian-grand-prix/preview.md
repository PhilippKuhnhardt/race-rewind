### Championship standings

Vettel leads Hamilton by seven points, with Bottas third. Ferrari leads Mercedes in the Constructors' Championship.

### Previous race

At Bahrain, Vettel won from Hamilton — Bottas had taken maiden pole by two hundredths of a second over Hamilton, led early, but allowed Hamilton through with ten laps remaining. Verstappen retired early with a brake failure. A collision between Sainz and Stroll brought out the safety car; Vandoorne failed to take the start after a pre-race power unit failure.

### Between-race developments

Carlos Sainz Jr. carries a three-place grid penalty for causing the collision with Lance Stroll at Bahrain. Stoffel Vandoorne has a fifteen-place grid penalty for exceeding his power unit component quota.

### Entrants

Sergey Sirotkin drives for Renault in the first free practice session, filling in for Nico Hülkenberg.
