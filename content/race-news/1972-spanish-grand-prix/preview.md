### Championship standings

Hulme leads the championship after winning South Africa.

### Previous race

At Kyalami, Hailwood rose from fourth on the grid to challenge Stewart for the lead before his rear suspension collapsed on lap 28. Stewart then dominated until a gearbox failure on lap 45 handed the lead to Fittipaldi; Hulme passed the struggling Lotus to win, over 14 seconds clear. Revson finished third — his first podium.

### Between-race developments

Carlos Reutemann injured his ankle in a Formula 2 race at Thruxton the previous weekend and misses Spain. Wilson Fittipaldi Júnior replaces him at Brabham.

### Entrants

Wilson Fittipaldi Júnior's entry alongside his brother Emerson marks the first time two brothers have raced simultaneously in a Formula One World Championship event. Ickx takes a dominant pole position, seven tenths of a second clear of Hulme and Emerson Fittipaldi; Stewart starts fourth.

### Milestones

This is the 200th Formula One World Championship Grand Prix, counting from the 1950 British Grand Prix and excluding the Indianapolis 500 races held between 1950 and 1960.
