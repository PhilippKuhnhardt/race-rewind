### Championship standings

Andretti leads the championship, with the Lotus pair of Andretti and Peterson pulling away from the rest of the field. Lauda's fifth engine failure of the season at Germany has left him further adrift.

### Previous race

Andretti won the German Grand Prix at Hockenheim for his fifth victory of the season, with Peterson's gearbox failing mid-race to leave Scheckter second and Laffite third. Jones had been running third before retiring with fuel vaporisation. Lauda was passed by Jones before suffering yet another engine failure — his fifth engine retirement in eleven rounds.

### Entrants

Jean-Pierre Jabouille qualified his turbocharged Renault third at Germany; the turbo is beginning to show consistent one-lap pace. This is Lauda's home race; the Österreichring crowd is heavily partisan in his favour.
