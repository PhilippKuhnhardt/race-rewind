### Championship standings

Hawthorn leads with 30 points, Moss is second on 24, and Brooks is third on 16.

### Previous race

At the Nürburgring, Peter Collins was fatally injured on lap 11 when his Ferrari ran wide in the Pflanzgarten section, struck a ditch and flipped into the air. He was thrown from the car and died in hospital later that day. Brooks won the race for Vanwall ahead of the Coopers of Salvadori and Trintignant.

### Between-race developments

Following Collins' death, Ferrari has brought in sports car driver Olivier Gendebien to join the team for the remaining rounds.

### Circuit

Portugal hosts a World Championship Grand Prix for the first time. The race is run on the Circuito da Boavista, a street circuit in Porto.

### Practice

Moss and Hawthorn are covered by just 0.05 seconds in qualifying, with the Vanwall on pole position. Stuart Lewis-Evans completes the Vanwall front row. Rain before the start leaves the roads wet.
