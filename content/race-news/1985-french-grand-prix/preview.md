### Championship standings

Alboreto leads the Drivers' Championship, with Prost nine points behind after Detroit — as far from the title as he has been all year.

### Previous race

At Detroit, Keke Rosberg won from Stefan Johansson and Alboreto. The street circuit was brutal on brakes and tyres in fierce heat and humidity — Prost, Mansell, and Senna all crashed at the same corner. Stefan Bellof drove brilliantly to fourth in his Tyrrell-Cosworth, scoring the final points for the Ford-Cosworth DFV engine until 1988.

### Between-race developments

François Hesnault has been dropped from Brabham after four disappointing results and replaced by Swiss driver Marc Surer.

### Entrants

This weekend Tyrrell become the last Formula One team to switch from a naturally aspirated engine to a turbo. Martin Brundle drives the new Renault-powered Tyrrell 014, while Stefan Bellof continues with the Cosworth-powered 012. This is the last race at the full Paul Ricard circuit layout — from 1986 a shorter 3.813 km configuration will be used.
