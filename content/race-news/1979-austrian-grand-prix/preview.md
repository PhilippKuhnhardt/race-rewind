### Previous race

Jones won at Hockenheim with Regazzoni second — a Williams one-two — and Laffite third. Jabouille led early but outbraked himself and spun off chasing Jones on the seventh lap. Jones ran with a slow puncture for the final twenty laps but Regazzoni was ordered to stay behind.

### Championship standings

Williams have now won two successive races at Silverstone and Hockenheim. Scheckter continues to lead the championship overall.

### Entrants

Derek Daly is brought into Tyrrell as stand-in for Jean-Pierre Jarier, who is still unwell. ATS has undergone a restructuring: former Grand Prix driver Vic Elford becomes team manager and the team arrives with a new D2 chassis designed by Nigel Stroud. The rest of the entry is unchanged from Hockenheim.

### Milestones

This is the 50th Grand Prix start for a Canadian driver.
