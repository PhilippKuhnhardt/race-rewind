### Previous race

Brabham won at Porto ahead of McLaren and Clark, clinching the World Championship with two rounds remaining. Brabham is champion for the second consecutive year.

### Between-race developments

Lotus, BRM, and Cooper have all withdrawn from the Italian Grand Prix, citing the fragility, extreme roughness, and poor construction of the concrete banking at Monza. Formula 2 cars are admitted to the entry list to fill the grid. Ferrari, the privateers, and Porsche compete.
