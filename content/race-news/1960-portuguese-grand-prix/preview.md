### Championship standings

Brabham leads the championship with a commanding advantage, having won the last three rounds in succession. A victory at Porto would give him the title with two races remaining.

### Previous race

Brabham won at Silverstone ahead of Surtees and Ireland. Graham Hill drove through the field to lead on lap 55 before brake problems forced him to spin at Copse and retire.

### Championship permutations

Brabham can clinch his second consecutive World Championship at this race.

### Between-race developments

Stirling Moss returns to competition for the first time since breaking both legs in practice at Spa. He missed the French and British Grands Prix during his recovery.

### Milestones

This is the last Portuguese Grand Prix for 24 years; the race does not return to the calendar until 1984.

### Practice

Henry Taylor crashed during practice and injured his arm. Jim Clark also had an accident in practice — an unusual occurrence for the Lotus driver.
