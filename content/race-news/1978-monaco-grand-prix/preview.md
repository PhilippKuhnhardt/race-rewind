### Championship standings

Reutemann and Andretti are level on 18 points at the top of the standings. Peterson and Depailler follow on 14, Lauda on 10.

### Previous race

Reutemann won the United States Grand Prix West in Long Beach, taking over the lead from Ferrari teammate Villeneuve when the Canadian crashed out after contact with a backmarker. Andretti finished second, Depailler third. Lauda retired with an electrical failure while running second.

### Between-race developments

The Japanese Grand Prix, which had been scheduled for 16 April at the Suzuka Circuit, was cancelled on safety and financial grounds before it could take place. The Monaco slot moves accordingly on the calendar.
