### Championship standings

At the halfway point of the season, Prost leads with 57 points. Senna is second with 45, Hill third with 28, and Schumacher fourth with 24. Williams lead the Constructors' Championship with 85 points, 37 ahead of McLaren's 48. Prost's winning margin over Senna has grown to 12 points.

### Previous race

At Magny-Cours, Prost won from second on the grid to take his fifth victory of the season. Hill led the first 26 laps from pole before Prost passed during the mid-race stops. Schumacher passed Senna in traffic to finish third, with Senna fourth. Prost became the first Formula One driver to record 100 career podium finishes.

### Milestones

Prost has 49 career wins entering Silverstone. A victory would make him the first driver in Formula One history to reach 50. This is the second British race of the season, after the European Grand Prix at Donington Park in April. With Mansell now racing in America, the home crowd has taken Damon Hill to their hearts.
