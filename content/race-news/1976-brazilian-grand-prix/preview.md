### Entrants

James Hunt starts his first race for McLaren, having replaced Emerson Fittipaldi over the winter. Fittipaldi lines up for his debut with Copersucar — his brother Wilson's team. Wolf-Williams Racing and Ligier both make their World Championship debuts this weekend. Mario Andretti and Ronnie Peterson share the Lotus garage. Ingo Hoffmann makes his Formula One World Championship debut.
