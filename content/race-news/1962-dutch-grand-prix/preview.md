### Entrants

Lotus arrives with the new Lotus 25, the first Formula One car built around an aluminium monocoque rather than a conventional space frame — a significant departure from established practice. Lola makes its Formula One World Championship debut with the Mk4, fielded privately by Reg Parnell for John Surtees.

### Milestones

The Dutch Grand Prix hosts the World Championship season opener for the first time and is awarded the honorary designation of the 22nd European Grand Prix.
