### Championship standings

Clark has won four consecutive races — Belgium, Holland, France, and Britain — and his lead over the field is substantial. The Nürburgring is the first circuit this season to seriously test his dominance.

### Previous race

Clark won at Silverstone from pole, bogging down at the start before retaking the lead within four laps. Gurney had been fighting for second when his engine blew on lap 60, spreading oil across the circuit. Hill ran third before running low on fuel, letting Surtees through to second before coasting home.
