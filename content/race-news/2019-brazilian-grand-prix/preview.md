### Championship standings

Hamilton is the 2019 World Drivers' Champion, clinching the title with second place at Austin. Bottas is second in the championship. The Constructors' Championship was secured at Japan.

### Previous race

Bottas won the United States Grand Prix from pole, with Hamilton second and Verstappen third. Hamilton's second-place finish was enough to clinch his sixth World Drivers' Championship, making him the outright second most accomplished Formula One driver in terms of championship wins behind Michael Schumacher. It was also Hamilton's 150th podium.

### Entrants

Nicholas Latifi drives in the first practice session for Williams, replacing Kubica.

### Penalties

Leclerc carries a 10-place grid penalty for exceeding his power unit component quota.
