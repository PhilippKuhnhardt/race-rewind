### Championship standings

Prost leads the championship after three wins in five races. Senna is second, having taken back points at Spain.

### Previous race

At the Circuit de Catalunya, Prost won from pole. Hill led the opening lap after a strong start but Prost took over on lap 11 and managed the pace. Hill retired with engine failure on lap 41 while closing in. Schumacher made a challenge on Senna but ran off the track at the final corner after having to avoid the smoking Lotus of Zanardi, ending his threat.

### Milestones

Senna arrives at Monaco having won here in 1987, 1989, 1990, 1991 and 1992 — five victories, equalling Graham Hill's record. A sixth win would break the record outright and set a new all-time mark for victories at a single Formula One circuit.
