### Championship standings

Senna's win in Hungary was his sixth of the season and moved him level on points with Prost. For the first time all year, neither McLaren driver leads outright — the championship is tied going into Spa.

### Between-race developments

Enzo Ferrari died on 14 August at the age of 90. This is the first race since the death of the founder of Scuderia Ferrari.

Nigel Mansell, still recovering from chickenpox after racing against medical advice in Hungary, is unable to start. Martin Brundle replaces him at Williams for this race.
