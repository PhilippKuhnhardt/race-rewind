### Championship standings

Prost leads the championship with seven wins from ten races. Hill is second in the standings but has been denied his first victory in back-to-back races — an engine failure at Silverstone and a tyre blow-out on the penultimate lap at Hockenheim.

### Previous race

At Hockenheim, Hill led throughout and appeared to be cruising to his first win when his left rear tyre deflated on the penultimate lap. Prost — who had served a stop-go penalty for overtaking under yellow flags and was struck by Patrese during the race — recovered to inherit victory. Prost called the penalty "a scandal" at the press conference.

### Milestones

Damon Hill is the son of two-time World Champion Graham Hill. A win at the Hungaroring would make him the first son of a Formula One World Champion to win a Grand Prix.
