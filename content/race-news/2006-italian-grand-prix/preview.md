### Championship standings

Alonso extended his lead to 12 points after Turkey despite Schumacher finishing on the podium. Ferrari also closed to within two points of Renault in the Constructors' Championship — the tightest the gap has been all season — making Monza a critical round for both titles.

### Previous race

Massa took his first career Formula One victory in Turkey, with Alonso second — finishing a tenth of a second ahead of Schumacher in a battle that continued to the final corners of the final lap. Räikkönen was eliminated on the opening lap after Speed's Toro Rosso clipped his rear tyre, causing a puncture; damaged bodywork on the following lap sent him off at Turn 4. A safety car was deployed on lap 13 after Liuzzi stalled at the exit of Turn 1.

### Milestones

Sebastian Vettel makes only his second Formula One race weekend appearance as BMW Sauber's Friday test driver, having set the fastest time in one session at Turkey. Vettel set the fastest time in both Friday practice sessions there.
