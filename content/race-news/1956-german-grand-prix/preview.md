### Championship standings

Collins leads Fangio by a single point — two Ferrari teammates separated at the top of the championship, with Behra a further three points behind in third.

### Previous race

Fangio won at Silverstone. Collins finished second in a shared drive with de Portago and Behra was third. The result placed Collins ahead of his teammate by one point going into the Nürburgring.

### Entrants

No British teams are entered. The field is drawn entirely from Italian constructors — Ferrari and Maserati — with French Gordinis at the rear. Among those in attendance is 18-year-old Juan Carlos — later King of Spain — present at the circuit in support of his relative Alfonso de Portago.

### Milestones

The Nürburgring's lap record has stood for 17 years, set by Mercedes driver Hermann Lang in 1939.

### Practice

Fangio takes pole, three tenths of a second ahead of teammate Collins.
