### Championship standings

Sebastian Vettel leads Lewis Hamilton by nine points in the Drivers' Championship. Mercedes hold a one-point lead over Ferrari in the Constructors'.

### Previous race

Ricciardo won in China with a two-stop strategy, pitting for fresh softs under a safety car with twenty laps remaining while the leaders stayed out on used mediums. He passed both Ferraris and the Mercedes to take the lead. Vettel finished eighth after contact with Verstappen late in the race, cutting his championship lead.

### Track notes

The race has been moved from its June slot to April to avoid clashing with celebrations marking the centenary of the Azerbaijani republic. The vacancy in June is filled by the returning French Grand Prix.

### Tyre choices

Pirelli brings the soft (yellow), supersoft (red), and ultrasoft (purple) compounds.
