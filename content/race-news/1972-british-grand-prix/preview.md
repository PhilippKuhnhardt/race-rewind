### Previous race

Stewart won at Charade after championship leader Amon suffered a puncture from the volcanic debris and lost 50 seconds in the pits. Fittipaldi finished second; Amon charged back from ninth to take third, shattering the circuit lap record.

### Between-race developments

Denny Hulme is recovering from a high-speed crash suffered during a Can-Am race the previous week. He takes part but qualifies down in eleventh.

### Entrants

The race is formally named the John Player Grand Prix. Ickx takes pole ahead of Fittipaldi and Revson; Stewart and Amon, both having crashed in practice and qualified in spare cars, start fourth and seventeenth. Frank Williams attempts to run a new Politoys FX3 chassis alongside his March entries.

### Milestones

Arturo Merzario makes his Formula One debut with Ferrari.
