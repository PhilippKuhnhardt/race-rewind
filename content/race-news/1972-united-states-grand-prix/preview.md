### Championship standings

The championship is decided. Fittipaldi is the 1972 World Champion.

### Previous race

At Mosport, Peterson led from third on the grid before losing the lead to Stewart after a mistake. Fittipaldi fell back with nose damage. Peterson was later disqualified for receiving a push back to the pits. Stewart won ahead of Revson and Hulme.

### Entrants

John Surtees has vacated his race seat; Tim Schenken drives the Surtees entry. Jody Scheckter makes his Formula One debut in a third McLaren, starting from eighth on the grid. The $275,000 prize fund attracts a field of 31 entries.

Rain and cold winds hampered qualifying; Friday's times determine the grid. Stewart takes pole in 1:40.481; Revson and Hulme start second and third. Goodyear teams hold a clear advantage in the cold conditions — Firestone's teams are running stock originally prepared for the Austrian Grand Prix. Peterson crashed heavily in Saturday's wet practice; his mechanics rebuilt the car overnight and he starts from 26th.

### Milestones

Jody Scheckter makes his Formula One World Championship debut.
