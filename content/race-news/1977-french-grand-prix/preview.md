### Championship standings

Scheckter leads the Drivers' Championship at the halfway point of the season.

### Previous race

At Anderstorp, Andretti dominated until a fuel metering problem two laps from the end handed the lead — and his first career win — to Jacques Laffite. The Ligier-Matra's victory was the first all-French win in World Championship history. Mass was second for McLaren and Reutemann third.

### Entrants

Patrick Tambay makes his Formula 1 debut at Dijon-Prenois.

### Track changes

The French Grand Prix moves to the extended Dijon-Prenois circuit, switching from Circuit Paul Ricard under the event-sharing arrangement between the two venues.
