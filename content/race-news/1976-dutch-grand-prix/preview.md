### Championship standings

Ferrari did not enter Austria, where Watson won for Penske. Hunt finished fourth. Lauda still leads the championship without having raced.

### Previous race

Watson won in Austria for his first Formula One victory and Penske's first and only win. Laffite finished second and Nilsson third. Hunt was fourth. With Lauda absent and Ferrari absent, the race attracted smaller crowds than usual.

### Between-race developments

Lauda has recovered from his injuries and is planning to return to racing later this season. Ferrari are back this weekend, though with only one car for Regazzoni — Lauda is not yet ready to race.

### Milestones

The weekend is overshadowed by the death of track marshal Ron Lenderink during a touring car support race. He was 29 years old. This is Hunt's 29th birthday weekend.

This is the 10th Grand Prix start for Wolf-Williams.
