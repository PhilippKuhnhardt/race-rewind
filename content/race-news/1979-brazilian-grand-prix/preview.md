### Previous race

Laffite won in Argentina with Depailler following him home, giving Ligier a dominant one-two. A massive crash at the fast esses on the opening lap eliminated Scheckter, Watson, Piquet, Tambay and Andretti and forced a red-flag restart. Piquet was taken to hospital with injuries. Reutemann drove a stirring race in front of his home crowd to finish second, with Watson third after switching to his spare car.

### Championship standings
