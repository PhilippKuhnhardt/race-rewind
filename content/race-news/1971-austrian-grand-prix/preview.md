### Championship standings

Stewart leads the championship with 51 points. Ickx is second with 19, Peterson third with 16.

### Previous race

Germany: Stewart and Cevert gave Tyrrell a one-two at the Nürburgring, with Regazzoni third after Ickx spun out attempting to keep pace with Stewart. A crowd of around 375,000 watched on the rebuilt circuit.

### Between-race developments

Peter Gethin has moved from McLaren to BRM, ahead of this race.

### Championship permutations
