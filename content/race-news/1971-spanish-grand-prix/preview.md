### Previous race

South Africa: Mario Andretti won at Kyalami on his Ferrari debut, with Stewart second and Regazzoni third — the first podium finish for Tyrrell as a constructor. Howden Ganley made his Formula One debut for BRM.

### Track changes

The Spanish Grand Prix moves from Jarama to Montjuïc Park in Barcelona, rotating between the two circuits as arranged. The race is scheduled for the morning.

### Milestones

Firestone introduce slick tyres to Formula One for the first time, based on their experience in American open-wheel racing. This is the first Formula One race to be run on slicks.
