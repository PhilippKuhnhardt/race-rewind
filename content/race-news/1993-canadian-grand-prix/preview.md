### Championship standings

Prost leads the championship after Spain, his third win of the season. Senna is second having scored at Monaco; the gap between them is tightening.

### Previous race

At Monaco, Prost jumped the start and received a stop-go penalty, dropping to last and a lap down after stalling during the pit stop. He spent the rest of the race climbing through the field. Schumacher led comfortably before retiring with a hydraulics problem on lap 33. Berger was penalised for a tyre wall contact and later retired after colliding with Hill on lap 71 while trying to overtake. Senna won for the fifth consecutive year at Monaco.

### Milestones

Senna's Monaco win was his sixth at the circuit in total, breaking the all-time record for victories at a single Formula One venue that he had shared with Graham Hill. It was also his fifth consecutive win at Monaco, surpassing Hill's record of five wins there in all.
