### Championship standings

Senna still leads the championship after winning four of the first five races.

### Previous race

At Mexico City, Patrese won for Williams with Mansell second — their first 1-2 of the season. De Cesaris finished fourth after pushing his Jordan over the line when the throttle failed on the final lap; the stewards ruled he had not broken the rules by starting to push after the race ended.

### Between-race developments

Footwork have abandoned the disastrous Porsche V12 engine project and are returning to Ford power. Ferrari introduce a new car this weekend, the 643.

### Track changes

The French Grand Prix moves to Circuit de Nevers Magny-Cours in central France. It is the first time the race has been held at Magny-Cours. The previous venue, Circuit Paul Ricard near Marseille, has hosted the race for most of the past two decades.
