### Championship standings

Alonso leads Räikkönen by 24 points and Schumacher by 40 points. In the Constructors' Championship, Renault lead McLaren by nine points and Ferrari by 44 points.

### Previous race

Räikkönen won Turkey from pole, leading every lap. Montoya ran second until the penultimate lap, when a collision with Jordan's Tiago Monteiro damaged his diffuser and he ran straight on at Turn 8, allowing Alonso through in second. Schumacher and Webber both retired after a collision on lap 14 damaged the Ferrari's rear suspension.

### Between-race developments

Williams driver Nick Heidfeld suffers severe headaches following a heavy crash during a test session at Monza during the week. He participates in Friday free practice sessions but withdraws before Saturday, citing his condition. Antônio Pizzonia replaces him from the first Saturday practice session.
