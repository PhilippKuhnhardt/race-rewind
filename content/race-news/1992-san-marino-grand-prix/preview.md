### Previous race

Mansell won in Spain for his fourth consecutive victory, though for the first time this season Williams did not score a 1–2 — Patrese spun out on lap 20 while lapping a backmarker, leaving Schumacher second and Alesi third.

### Qualifying

Mansell takes his fifth consecutive pole, breaking the streak of seven consecutive Imola poles held by Senna. He qualifies over a second clear of Patrese, who crashed at Tamburello two weeks before the race. The McLarens of Senna and Berger fill the second row, with the Benettons of Schumacher and Brundle on the third. Brundle finally gets off the mark after four consecutive non-scores. Häkkinen, Chiesa, Hill, and van de Poele all fail to qualify — the only time Häkkinen misses the grid all season.
