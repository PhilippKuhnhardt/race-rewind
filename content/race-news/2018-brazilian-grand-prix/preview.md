### Championship standings

Lewis Hamilton has secured the 2018 Drivers' Championship. The Constructors' title is still undecided: Mercedes lead Ferrari, and Ferrari must outscore Mercedes by thirteen points or more to prevent Mercedes winning the title after this race.

### Previous race

Verstappen won in Mexico by 17.3 seconds. Hamilton finished fourth, which was enough to clinch his fifth Drivers' Championship. Vettel came second.

### Track notes

Following Felipe Massa's retirement at the end of 2017, this is the first Brazilian Grand Prix without a Brazilian driver.
