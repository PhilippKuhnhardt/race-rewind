### Championship standings

Fittipaldi and Hulme are tied on 15 points after three rounds.

### Previous race

In Spain, Ickx took a dominant pole but the race was turned by light rain around lap 30. Fittipaldi moved through to lead and held on for victory; Stewart spun off and retired. Ickx finished second, setting a new lap record and lapping his Ferrari teammate Regazzoni in third.

### Track changes

The Monaco circuit has been substantially modified for safety. The pit lane moves to the harbour front, between the old chicane and Tabac corner, and a new chicane is inserted near Tabac.

### Weather

Rain falls before the start, and the field departs into heavy spray.

### Milestones

Fittipaldi takes pole position — his first in Formula One and the first pole by a Brazilian driver in the World Championship. The Ferraris of Ickx and Regazzoni start second and third; Beltoise and Gethin qualify an unexpected fourth and fifth for BRM.
