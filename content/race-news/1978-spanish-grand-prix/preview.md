### Championship standings

Andretti leads the championship after the Belgian Grand Prix. Depailler and Reutemann follow, with Peterson moving up after a strong Belgium result and Lauda fifth.

### Previous race

Andretti won Belgium with the new Lotus 79 in dominant fashion, pulling away from the field to give Lotus a 1–2 with Peterson second and Reutemann third. Lauda was hit by Scheckter at the start and retired on the opening lap. The Lotus 79's debut pace — Andretti took pole comfortably and led every lap — signals a significant shift in competitiveness.

### Entrants

Peterson drives the Lotus 79 for the first time this weekend, having used the older Lotus 78 at Zolder. Both Lotus cars are now equipped with the ground-effect car.

Emilio de Villota enters a private McLaren for the Spanish round only.
