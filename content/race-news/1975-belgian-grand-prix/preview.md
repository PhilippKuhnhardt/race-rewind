### Championship standings

Fittipaldi leads the championship after Monaco.

### Previous race

At Monaco, Lauda gave the new Ferrari 312T its first win, breaking a 20-year Ferrari drought at the principality. Fittipaldi came second, with Pace third. It was the last Monaco Grand Prix weekend for Graham Hill as any kind of participant — he had already announced his retirement as a driver after failing to qualify.

### Entrants

Tony Brise makes his debut with Graham Hill's Embassy Racing team. Mario Andretti is absent from the field, competing at the Indianapolis 500 in the United States.

### Practice

Ronnie Peterson parks his car at the edge of the track rather than risk a puncture by pulling into the gravel trap — and is subsequently issued a parking ticket by circuit officials.
