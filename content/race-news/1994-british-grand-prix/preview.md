### Championship standings

Going into Silverstone, Schumacher leads the Drivers' Championship with 66 points from a possible 70 — six wins and a second in seven races. Hill is second on 29 points, Berger third on 17, Alesi fourth on 13. Benetton lead the Constructors' Championship on 67 points, Ferrari second on 36, Williams third on 31.

### Previous race

At France, Schumacher won from third on the grid for his sixth victory in seven races. Hill started from pole and led until losing the position at the pit stops. Alesi spun on lap 42 and was collected by Barrichello; both retired. Mansell was running third but his transmission failed on lap 46. Berger finished second, Frentzen fourth.

### Between-race developments

Nigel Mansell has returned to his CART commitments in North America following France; David Coulthard is back in the second Williams alongside Hill.
