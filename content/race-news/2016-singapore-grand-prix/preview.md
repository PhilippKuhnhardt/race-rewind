### Championship standings

Hamilton leads Rosberg by 2 points entering Singapore.

### Previous race

Rosberg won Italy, taking advantage of a poor start by Hamilton to establish an early lead. Hamilton recovered from sixth at the start, passed both Ferraris by making one fewer pit stop, and finished second. Vettel was third, Räikkönen fourth.

### Penalties

Sebastian Vettel carries a five-place grid penalty for an unscheduled gearbox change plus a twenty-place penalty for using additional power unit elements. Sergio Pérez carries a combined eight-place penalty: five places for failing to slow for yellow flags and three more for passing Gutiérrez under yellows. Romain Grosjean has a five-place penalty for a new gearbox.
