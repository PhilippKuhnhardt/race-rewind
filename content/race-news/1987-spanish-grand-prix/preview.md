### Championship standings

Piquet leads by 18 points over Senna and 24 over Mansell after Portugal, with three races remaining.

### Previous race

At Estoril, a multi-car collision at the start eliminated several drivers, including Danner whose Zakspeed was the only car unable to restart. Berger led for most of the race in his Ferrari until he spun under pressure from Prost with three laps to go. Prost came through to take his 28th Grand Prix victory, breaking Jackie Stewart's all-time record. Prost also equalled Niki Lauda's record of 54 career podium finishes.

### Entrants

Scuderia Coloni returns after missing Portugal. Franco Forini races for Osella for a third time. The expanded entry brings 28 cars to Jerez.
