### Championship standings

Hamilton leads the Drivers' Championship by 3 points from Alonso. Following McLaren's exclusion from the Constructors' Championship after being found guilty in the espionage case, Ferrari lead the standings by 57 points from BMW Sauber. Without the exclusion, McLaren would have been leading.

### Previous race

Alonso won in Italy from pole, with Hamilton second — a fourth McLaren one-two of the season. It was McLaren's eighth victory at Monza and Alonso became the first Spaniard to win the Italian Grand Prix.

### Between-race developments

McLaren were excluded from the Constructors' Championship following the World Motor Sport Council's ruling that they had unlawfully used confidential Ferrari technical information. The team will receive no constructor points for this or any subsequent race.

### Milestones

This race marks the 100th Grand Prix start for Mark Webber of Red Bull Racing.

### Practice

Ferrari try a new traffic light system from the pits for the first time during practice.
