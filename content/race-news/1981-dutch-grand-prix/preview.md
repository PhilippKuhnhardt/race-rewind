### Championship standings

Piquet is six points behind championship leader Reutemann heading into Zandvoort. Laffite, who won in Austria, remains within striking distance further back.

### Previous race

At the Österreichring, Laffite won after Prost — who had led comfortably — retired from the front with a suspension failure. Arnoux finished second; Piquet third. Reutemann coasted over the finish line with a dead engine to take fifth.

### Between-race developments

Fittipaldi return to the grid after missing Austria through a lack of engines; the team has switched to Pirelli tyres. Andrea de Cesaris qualifies 13th for McLaren, but the team withdraws his entry, citing concern that he will damage another car. His season has included 19 accidents, eight of them during races — without once injuring himself.
