### Championship standings

After five races, Prost leads the championship with 45 points to Senna's 33. McLaren has won every race this season and leads the Constructors' by a wide margin — 78 points to Ferrari's 27.

### Practice

Ivan Capelli crashed into the pit wall during Saturday practice and broke a bone in his left foot. Nicola Larini was promoted to replace him on the grid.

### Weather

Race day is expected to be warm and sunny with temperatures reaching 33 °C. The circuit surface has been badly damaged by a Trans-Am race held on Saturday, which tore up the road surface significantly. Hasty concrete repairs have created an extremely abrasive track, leading teams to reconsider whether tyre stops will be necessary.
