### Championship standings

Lewis Hamilton leads Sebastian Vettel by seventeen points in the Drivers' Championship. Mercedes lead Ferrari by eight points in the Constructors'.

### Previous race

Hamilton won in Germany from fourteenth on the grid after race leader Vettel crashed out in light rain on lap 52, striking the wall at turn 13. The incident triggered a safety car; Hamilton, having made one fewer pit stop, inherited the lead and held it to the finish. Bottas completed a Mercedes 1–2, Räikkönen third.

### Between-race developments

Ferrari chairman Sergio Marchionne died on 25 July following complications from surgery. Ferrari and engine customer teams Haas and Sauber are running black armbands and black stripes on their cars this weekend as a tribute.
