### Previous race

Fittipaldi won in Brazil, with Stewart second and Hulme third.

### Entrants

Shadow make their Formula One championship debut at Kyalami with Jackie Oliver and George Follmer, the 1972 Can-Am champion. South African driver Eddie Keizan also makes his championship debut on home ground.

### Milestones

Scuderia Ferrari line up for their 200th world championship start as a constructor. In those 200 races, Ferrari have won 49 Grands Prix, collected 183 podium finishes, and taken six Drivers' titles.

### Practice

Stewart suffered a dramatic brake failure in practice, spinning backwards through three layers of chain-link fence at Kyalami. He was unhurt.
