### Championship standings

Senna leads after winning both opening rounds.

### Previous race

At Interlagos, Senna won his home race for the first time in eight Formula 1 seasons. He spent the final laps nursing a failing gearbox down to a single gear, nearly stalling several times, before winning by 2.9 seconds. The effort left him with muscle cramps and a fever; he had to be lifted from the car and transported to the podium in the medical car, barely able to raise the trophy.
