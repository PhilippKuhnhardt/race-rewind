### Previous race

At Jarama, Peterson led from the start on a damp, drying track. Ferrari got Lauda in and out for slick tyres in 35 seconds, enabling him to take the lead and hold it to the flag for his first career victory — Ferrari's 50th Grand Prix win, ending a record-setting 22-race winning streak for Ford-powered cars. Regazzoni completed a Ferrari 1–2. Merzario was running a strong fourth before hitting the wall.

### Circuit

The Belgian Grand Prix moves from Zolder to Nivelles-Baulers. This is the second and last time the race will be held at this circuit.

### Entrants

Tom Pryce makes his Formula One World Championship debut with the newly formed Token Racing team. Teddy Pilette, Leo Kinnunen, and Gérard Larrousse also make their championship debuts this weekend. This is also Token's first World Championship start and Surtees' 50th Grand Prix.

### Qualifying

Clay Regazzoni takes pole position for Ferrari, with Jody Scheckter's Tyrrell second and Niki Lauda third.
