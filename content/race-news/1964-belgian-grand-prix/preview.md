### Championship standings

Jim Clark leads the championship after winning at Zandvoort. Hill's misfire at the Dutch Grand Prix cost him points he had looked likely to recover after Monaco, and Clark now carries the momentum.

### Previous race

At Zandvoort, Clark reached Tarzan corner ahead of Hill and never looked back. Gurney retired on lap 22, and Hill's BRM developed a misfire shortly after, leaving Clark to win ahead of Surtees and Arundell.

### Milestones

This is the 50th Formula One World Championship race for a BRM-powered car. Clark won this race in both 1962 and 1963, making him the only driver to have won at Spa-Francorchamps in consecutive seasons.
