### Championship standings

Verstappen leads the championship with 93 points, 6 ahead of Pérez. Alonso is third, 33 off the lead. Red Bull lead the Constructors' with 180 points; Aston Martin are second with 87, Mercedes third with 76.

### Previous race

At Baku, Pérez won both the sprint and the Grand Prix, with Leclerc taking second in the race — Ferrari's first podium of the season. Verstappen finished third in the race after losing the lead to Pérez during the pit-stop phase. The sprint shootout ended prematurely when Sargeant crashed, and Norris was unable to participate in SQ3 after exhausting his allocation of soft tyres under the new mandatory compound rules.

### Tyre choices
