### Championship standings

Alonso leads Räikkönen after winning France. Schumacher remains third following his Magny-Cours podium.

### Previous race

Alonso took his fifth win of the season at Magny-Cours, with Räikkönen second and Schumacher third. Räikkönen's engine penalty dropped him from third to thirteenth on the grid, but he charged through the field. Montoya retired from third with a hydraulics failure.

### Between-race developments

On 7 July, bombings in London kill 52 people and injure hundreds more. A minute of silence will be observed before the race at Silverstone as a mark of respect.

### Entrants

Patrick Friesacher drives his final race for Minardi. Jordan's Tiago Monteiro did not complete a qualifying lap in France after suffering an engine failure in Friday practice, and starts from the rear of the grid.
