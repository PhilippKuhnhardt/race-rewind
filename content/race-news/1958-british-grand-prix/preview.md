### Previous race

At Reims, Hawthorn won ahead of championship rival Moss and Ferrari teammate Wolfgang von Trips. Fangio finished fourth in what was his final Formula One race; on the last lap, Hawthorn eased off so that Fangio could unlap himself and complete the full race distance.

### Between-race developments

Ferrari driver Luigi Musso was fatally injured at the Courbe du Calvaire during the French Grand Prix when he ran wide and his car struck a ditch and somersaulted. He was airlifted to hospital with critical head injuries and died later that day.

Juan Manuel Fangio has retired from Formula One following the French race.

### Circuit

The British Grand Prix moves to Silverstone from Aintree, in keeping with the event-sharing arrangement between the two circuits.

### Entrants

Bernie Ecclestone enters a pair of Connaught B-Types for Ivor Bueb and Jack Fairman, with Ecclestone also named as a driver of the Fairman car in the event he needs to take over the entry.

### Practice

Three different British teams occupy the front row: Moss (Vanwall), Schell (BRM), and Salvadori (Cooper).
