### Previous race

Italy went to Piquet, who led from lap 4 and was never headed. Jones recovered from seventh to second, with Reutemann third — Jones's and Reutemann's podium finishes were enough for Williams to clinch the Constructors' Championship with two races remaining. The win gave Piquet the championship lead by one point from Jones.

### Championship

Piquet leads Jones by one point with two rounds remaining.

### Entrants

Vittorio Brambilla has retired from Formula One; Alfa Romeo replace him with Italian debutant Andrea de Cesaris. New Zealander Mike Thackwell makes his Grand Prix debut in a third Tyrrell entry — at 19 years and 182 days he becomes the youngest driver to start a Formula One World Championship race, breaking a record held for 19 years by the late Ricardo Rodríguez. American Kevin Cogan also makes his debut.
