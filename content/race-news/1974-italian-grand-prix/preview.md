### Previous race

At the Österreichring, Reutemann won from pole after Lauda dropped back with a misfiring engine and eventually retired. Hulme finished second — his last podium of his Formula One career — Hunt third. Regazzoni ran as high as second but suffered a slow puncture and pitted twice, recovering to fifth. Fittipaldi's engine blew up while running third, costing him valuable points.

### Between-race developments

David Hobbs completes his stint at McLaren after Germany and Austria. Jochen Mass moves up from Surtees to take over the second McLaren for the remainder of the season.

### Milestones

This weekend marks Lotus's 100th podium finish in Formula One.

### Qualifying

Lauda takes pole position, with the Brabhams of Reutemann and Carlos Pace filling the second and third grid slots.
