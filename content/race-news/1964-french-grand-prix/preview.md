### Championship standings

Clark leads the championship after back-to-back wins at Zandvoort and Spa. Hill remains close, though retirements have interrupted his rhythm.

### Previous race

At Spa, Gurney led most of the race and broke the lap record multiple times, building a 40-second advantage — but ran out of fuel without realising it. Hill took the lead as Gurney slowed, only to retire with a failing fuel pump on the final lap. McLaren was then on course for second until his engine cut out with less than a kilometre to go, the car rolling towards the downhill finish as Clark swept by to win. McLaren crossed second, Brabham third. Clark ran out of fuel on his cool-down lap and was brought back to the pits seated on his teammate's engine cover.
