### Championship standings

Hamilton leads Alonso by 7 points after the Hungary controversy. McLaren lead Ferrari by 19 points in the Constructors' Championship, despite McLaren being ineligible to score constructor points in Hungary.

### Previous race

Hamilton won a controversial Hungarian Grand Prix from pole. Räikkönen finished second. McLaren were stripped of constructor points for the race following the qualifying pit lane incident between their two drivers.

### Milestones

This is the third Formula One race held at the Istanbul Park. Both previous winners of the Turkish Grand Prix — Räikkönen in 2005 and Massa in 2006 — are now Ferrari teammates. The 2006 event was overshadowed by a $5,000,000 fine to the organisers for political bias, after they chose the President of the Turkish Cypriot state to present the winner's trophy.
