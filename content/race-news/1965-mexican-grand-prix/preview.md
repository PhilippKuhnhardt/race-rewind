### Championship standings

Clark is confirmed World Champion. Hill and Stewart are separated by points in the contest for second place in the Drivers' standings.

### Previous race

At Watkins Glen, Hill won for the third consecutive year — his second victory of the season. Clark took the lead on lap two but retired with a broken piston on lap 12. Hill built up a large lead, went off on a wet patch on lap 37 and lost the advantage, but recovered as the track dried. Gurney and Brabham both challenged and both went wide in turn at separate moments; Hill held on to win with Gurney second and Brabham third.

### Milestones

Mexico City is the final round of the 1.5-litre era. From 1966, Formula One moves to the new 3-litre engine formula, ending five seasons under the current regulations.
