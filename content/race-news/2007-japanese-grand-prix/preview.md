### Championship standings

Hamilton leads the Drivers' Championship by 2 points from Alonso, with Räikkönen 11 points further back in third. Ferrari are confirmed as Constructors' Champions — clinching the title at Belgium following McLaren's exclusion — and lead BMW Sauber by 71 points.

### Previous race

Räikkönen won at Spa for Ferrari, with Massa second and Alonso third. The result confirmed Ferrari's Constructors' Championship.

### Milestones

The Japanese Grand Prix returns to Fuji Speedway for the first time since 1977, ending a 20-year run at Suzuka. Three Japanese constructors — Toyota, Honda, and Super Aguri — and two Japanese drivers compete. Takuma Sato is 15th in the Drivers' Championship for Super Aguri; compatriot Sakon Yamamoto has yet to score for Spyker.

### Story tidbits

David Coulthard races with the helmet design of former World Rally Champion Colin McRae, who died two weeks before this race in a helicopter crash.
