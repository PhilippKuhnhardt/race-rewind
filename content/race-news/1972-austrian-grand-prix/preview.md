### Championship standings

Fittipaldi's championship lead was trimmed at the Nürburgring, where his gearbox failed and caused an engine fire. Ickx's dominant victory kept Ferrari's challenge alive.

### Previous race

Ickx led from pole at the Nürburgring and was out of sight of his rivals within two laps, setting new lap records throughout. Fittipaldi climbed to second before his gearbox blew on lap 10, triggering an engine fire. Stewart, stuck in fifth for much of the race, attempted to pass Regazzoni on the penultimate lap but their wheels touched and Stewart crashed out. The Ferraris finished first and second, Peterson third.
