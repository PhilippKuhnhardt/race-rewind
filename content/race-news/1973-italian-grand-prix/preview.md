### Previous race

Peterson won at the Österreichring with Stewart second and Pace third — Pace's first Formula One podium. Fittipaldi, who had been waved through by his teammate to keep his championship bid alive, retired on lap 48 when his fuel line came loose.

### Championship permutations

Stewart can clinch the Drivers' Championship at Monza if he finishes third or higher. A lower-points finish would require Fittipaldi to win and Cevert to finish on the podium to keep the title fight mathematically alive.

### Entrants

Jacky Ickx returns to Ferrari for the Italian Grand Prix, rejoining the team for their home race having left after the British Grand Prix.
