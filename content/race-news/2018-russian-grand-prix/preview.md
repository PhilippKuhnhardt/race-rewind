### Championship standings

Lewis Hamilton leads Sebastian Vettel by forty points in the Drivers' Championship. Mercedes lead Ferrari by thirty-seven points in the Constructors'.

### Previous race

Hamilton won in Singapore from pole, leading every lap ahead of Verstappen and Vettel.

### Track notes

The race has been moved from its April slot to September, filling the vacancy left by the discontinued Malaysian Grand Prix. The first DRS zone has been extended by one hundred metres for this event, with its beginning now positioned ninety-five metres before turn 1.

### Entrants

Artem Markelov replaces Carlos Sainz Jr. at Renault during the first practice session.
