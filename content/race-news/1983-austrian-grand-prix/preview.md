### Championship standings

Prost leads by nine points from Piquet. Tambay is third and Arnoux, with two wins in three races, is moving into contention in fourth. Ferrari leads the Constructors' Championship by three from Renault.

### Previous race

Arnoux won Germany for his second victory of the season. Tambay took pole but retired with engine failure after twelve laps. De Cesaris held on for second in the Alfa Romeo, with Patrese taking his first points of the year in third. Prost salvaged fourth but Piquet failed to score, allowing Prost to extend his lead.
