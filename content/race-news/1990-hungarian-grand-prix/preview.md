### Previous race

At Hockenheim, Senna won by six seconds over Nannini to retake the championship lead, four points clear of Prost. Mansell went off the road at the Ostkurve chicane on lap 14, damaging his car and retiring two laps later. Prost finished fourth.

### Championship standings

Senna leads Prost by four points, 48 to 44, at the start of the second half of the season.

### Between-race developments

Camel announced it is ending its sponsorship of Lotus at the end of 1990 and will sponsor Williams and Benetton from 1991.

### Qualifying

On the tight and twisty Hungaroring, Williams dominated qualifying for the first time this season. Boutsen took pole — the first and only pole position of his career — a mere 0.036 seconds ahead of team-mate Patrese. Berger was third, Senna fourth, with Prost back in eighth.
