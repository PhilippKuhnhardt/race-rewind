### Previous race

At Estoril, Ayrton Senna led the Portuguese Grand Prix from start to finish in torrential rain, lapping every car on the grid except Michele Alboreto in second. It was Senna's first Formula One victory and first pole position. Patrick Tambay and Elio de Angelis took third and fourth, both a lap down. The drive is already being spoken of as one of the great wet-weather performances in the sport.
