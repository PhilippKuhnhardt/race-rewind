### Previous race

At Monza, Ferrari dominated a race boycotted by the British factory teams. Phil Hill, Wolfgang von Trips, and Willy Mairesse filled the top three positions. With the championship already settled, the result was of limited significance to the title fight.

### Context

This is the only Formula One World Championship race ever held at Riverside International Raceway in California. The circuit replaces Sebring, which hosted the United States Grand Prix in 1959, after Sebring proved unprofitable. Riverside is the season finale.

### Milestones

This is the final championship round of the 2.5-litre formula era. From 1961, FIA regulations mandate a shift to 1.5-litre engines.
