### Previous race

Peterson won at Monza with Fittipaldi second and Revson third. Stewart, having started sixth, suffered a puncture that dropped him to twentieth on the first lap, but fought back to fourth — enough to clinch his third Drivers' Championship.

### Between-race developments

Three days after the Italian Grand Prix, Stewart announced his retirement from Formula One at the end of the season.

### Championship standings

Stewart has clinched the Drivers' Championship. The Manufacturers' Cup remains to be decided.

### Milestones

This is Stewart's 99th Formula One world championship start. Jody Scheckter's McLaren carries the number 0, the first time that number has appeared in a championship race.
