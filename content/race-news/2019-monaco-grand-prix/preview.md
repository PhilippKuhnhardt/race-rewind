### Championship standings

Hamilton holds a 7-point lead over Bottas. Mercedes leads Ferrari by 96 in the Constructors'.

### Previous race

Hamilton won Spain — his third consecutive Spanish Grand Prix victory — with Bottas second, completing a fifth Mercedes 1–2. Verstappen was third. Ferrari team orders saw Vettel and Leclerc swap positions twice, the pair finishing fourth and fifth.

### Between-race developments

Three-time world champion Niki Lauda died on 20 May at the age of 70, less than a week before this race, after a long period of ill health. All teams have paid tribute this weekend. Mercedes — where Lauda had been a key figure — have painted their halos red and added a red three-pointed star to the engine cover; they will run this tribute livery for the remainder of the season.

### Milestones

Kimi Räikkönen makes his 300th Formula One Grand Prix entry this weekend.
