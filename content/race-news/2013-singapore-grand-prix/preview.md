### Previous race

Sebastian Vettel won in Italy. Felipe Massa announced two days after the Italian race that he will leave Ferrari at the end of the season after eight years with the team.

### Between-race developments

Ferrari has confirmed that Kimi Räikkönen will replace Massa at the team for the 2014 season. Räikkönen, who is currently driving for Lotus, signed a deal to return to Ferrari — where he raced from 2007 to 2009 and won the 2007 World Championship.

### Track changes

The circuit layout has been revised for 2013 with the removal of the "Singapore Sling" chicane at Turn 10. The corner has been condensed to a single change in direction; the large kerbs that marked the chicane have been removed entirely.

### Tyre choices

Pirelli brings the medium (prime) and super-soft (option) compounds, compared with soft and super-soft at the 2012 race.
