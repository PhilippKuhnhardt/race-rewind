### Championship standings

Clark has won three of four races and holds a commanding lead. Hill and Stewart trail, with Stewart making an impression in his debut season despite starting the year as the team's number two driver.

### Previous race

At Charade, Clark took pole, led every lap, and set fastest lap — a Grand Slam — for his third win in four races. Stewart finished second, Surtees third. Bandini crashed on lap 36. Hill, starting from thirteenth, recovered to fifth.

### Milestones

This race marks the Formula One World Championship debut of British drivers John Rhodes, Alan Rollinson, and Brian Gubby.

### Track changes

The British Grand Prix returns to Silverstone this year, alternating with Brands Hatch under the two-circuit arrangement. Last year's race was held at Brands Hatch.
