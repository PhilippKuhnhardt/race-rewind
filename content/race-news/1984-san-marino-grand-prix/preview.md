### Championship standings

Prost leads the championship. Warwick moved into second place after Belgium, five points behind the Frenchman, after both McLarens failed to finish at Zolder.

### Previous race

Alboreto won at Zolder for Ferrari in only his third race for the team, becoming the first Italian driver to win for Ferrari since Ludovico Scarfiotti at the 1966 Italian Grand Prix. Warwick finished second, Arnoux third. Prost and Lauda both retired.

### Practice

A dispute between the Toleman team and tyre suppliers Pirelli left Ayrton Senna without any running in Friday practice. On Saturday, a misfire developed in his Hart 415T engine, preventing him from setting a competitive time. Senna failed to qualify for the race — the only time in his Formula One career this would happen.
