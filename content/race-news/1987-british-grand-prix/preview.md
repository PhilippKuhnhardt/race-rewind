### Championship standings

Ayrton Senna leads the Drivers' Championship by one point over Alain Prost after France, with Nelson Piquet three points behind Senna and Mansell six behind.

### Previous race

At Paul Ricard, Mansell won after passing Piquet with four laps to go following Piquet's error at Le Beausset. Piquet had led for most of the race but stalled in the pitlane during his second stop, losing nearly ten seconds. Senna held fourth throughout to retain the championship lead.

### Track changes

The Silverstone circuit has been modified: the Woodcote chicane has been removed and replaced with a new, slower chicane further down the Bridge straight.
