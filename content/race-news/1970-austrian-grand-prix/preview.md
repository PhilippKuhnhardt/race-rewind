### Previous race

Rindt won at Hockenheim for his fourth consecutive victory. Ickx was second, Hulme third. Brabham's engine failed on lap 4 after he had started 12th.

### Milestones

This is the first Grand Prix at the new Österreichring — the third Austrian Grand Prix overall and the second to count toward the World Championship. Jack Brabham makes his 122nd World Championship start, a new record.

### Qualifying

Rindt is on pole at his home circuit. Clay Regazzoni and Ickx are immediately behind on the front row. Ignazio Giunti starts fifth in the third Ferrari. Brabham starts eighth. Tim Schenken makes his Formula One debut this weekend.
