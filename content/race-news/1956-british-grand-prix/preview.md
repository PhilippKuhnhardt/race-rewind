### Previous race

Collins took his second consecutive victory at Reims, ahead of teammate Castellotti and Jean Behra. Fangio retired with a split fuel line on lap 40.

### Championship standings

Collins has won the last two races while Fangio has retired at both. The championship has shifted heading into a home race for three of the leading drivers.

### Entrants

BRM enters with their own chassis for the first time since 1951, making its championship debut at the British round. Tony Brooks makes his championship debut alongside Hawthorn in the BRM.

British manufacturer Emeryson makes its first Formula One race entry and start.

### Practice

Silverstone's wide circuit allows a four-car front row. Moss, Fangio, Hawthorn (BRM), and Collins share the front row — three British drivers and the defending champion.
