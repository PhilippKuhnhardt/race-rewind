The 70th running of the Monaco Grand Prix as a round of the Formula One World Championship, and the 81st time the event is held at the Circuit de Monaco across 24–26 May. This is the eighth round of the 2024 season.

### Championship standings

After the Emilia Romagna Grand Prix, Max Verstappen leads the Drivers' Championship with 161 points — 48 points ahead of Charles Leclerc in second and 54 ahead of Sergio Pérez in third. Red Bull Racing leads the Constructors' Championship with 267 points, from Ferrari (212) and McLaren (154).

### Previous race

At Imola, Verstappen took his eighth consecutive pole position and won the race by just 0.725 seconds over Lando Norris, who closed the gap from six seconds in the final 15 laps. Verstappen's eighth consecutive pole equalled Ayrton Senna's record. Sergio Pérez suffered his first Q2 elimination of the season, qualifying 11th and finishing eighth.
