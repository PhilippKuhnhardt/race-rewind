### Previous race

Hill won at Imola in an emotional result at the track where Senna died a year earlier. Alesi finished second, Berger third. Schumacher crashed leaving the pits on cold tyres and retired, while Coulthard was penalised for exceeding the pit lane speed limit. Mansell, in his McLaren debut, collided with Irvine during the race.

### Entrants

Mansell races at Catalunya for his second appearance with McLaren.

### Practice

Schumacher qualified on pole — his second of the season.
