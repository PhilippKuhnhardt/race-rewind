### Previous race

At Anderstorp, the two Tyrrells of Depailler and Scheckter dominated. Peterson retired early with a driveshaft failure, Regazzoni followed with gearbox problems. Scheckter held on from Depailler by 0.38 seconds to score his first Grand Prix win. Hunt took third for Hesketh — the team's first championship points. Graham Hill scored a championship point for the first time since 1972.

### Circuit

The Dutch Grand Prix has been moved forward from late July to mid-June, an earlier slot than on previous visits to Zandvoort.

### Qualifying

Niki Lauda takes his fourth pole position of the season, with teammate Regazzoni alongside. The McLarens of Fittipaldi and Hailwood fill the second row.
