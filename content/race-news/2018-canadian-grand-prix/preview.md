### Championship standings

Lewis Hamilton leads Sebastian Vettel by fourteen points in the Drivers' Championship. Mercedes hold a seventeen-point lead over Ferrari in the Constructors'.

### Previous race

Ricciardo won in Monaco despite developing an engine problem mid-race that left him severely down on power. He nursed the stricken car to the finish, with Vettel second and Hamilton third.

### Milestones

Fernando Alonso makes his 300th Formula One Grand Prix entry this weekend.

### Tyre choices

Pirelli brings the hypersoft, ultrasoft, and supersoft compounds.

### Practice

Verstappen was fastest across all three practice sessions, improving his time in each. In FP2, Grosjean struck a groundhog on the circuit, damaging his front wing and depleting Haas's supply of spare parts.
