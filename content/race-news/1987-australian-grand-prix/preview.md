### Previous race

At Suzuka, Ferrari ended a 38-race winless run — their first victory since the 1985 German Grand Prix. Mansell was absent after his qualifying crash; Piquet was crowned World Champion before the race started. Senna took second on the final lap.

### Between-race developments

Nigel Mansell is still recovering from his qualifying accident in Japan and will miss the race. Riccardo Patrese — who has already signed for Williams to partner Mansell in 1988 — has been given permission by Brabham owner Bernie Ecclestone to stand in for Mansell. Patrese's place at Brabham is taken by Stefano Modena, the 1987 Formula 3000 champion, making his Formula One debut.

### Entrants

This is Ayrton Senna's final race with Team Lotus, ending a two-year partnership. It is also Nelson Piquet's last race with Williams, as he moves to Lotus-Honda for 1988. Riccardo Patrese drives his final race for Brabham before joining Williams.

### Practice

Despite being ill during qualifying, Gerhard Berger took pole position by 0.7 seconds.
