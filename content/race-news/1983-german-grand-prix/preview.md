### Championship standings

Prost leads by six points from Piquet, with Tambay two further back. Renault leads the Constructors' Championship by three points from Ferrari.

### Previous race

Prost won Britain for his third victory of the season, passing both Ferraris before lap 20 on the fastest circuit of the year. Piquet moved up to second before the end, with Tambay third. Mansell took fourth in his first turbo race at home, ahead of Arnoux and Lauda. Spirit's debut ended on lap six with a fuel system problem on Johansson's Honda.
