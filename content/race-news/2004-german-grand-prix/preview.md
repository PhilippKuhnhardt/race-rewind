### Championship standings

Schumacher's 100 points — built from ten victories in eleven races — leave him 26 clear of Barrichello and 47 ahead of Button. Ferrari's 174 Constructors' points lead Renault on 79 and BAR on 67.

### Previous race

Schumacher started fourth at Silverstone and used the opening stint to set five consecutive fastest laps while the frontrunners pitted, emerging in the lead. A safety car period, caused by Trulli's heavy crash at Bridge — his rear suspension failed, rolling the car into the gravel — allowed Räikkönen to close, but the Finn could not match Schumacher's pace at the restart. Räikkönen finished second for McLaren's first podium of the season; Barrichello was third.

### Between-race developments

Marc Gené's stint covering for the injured Ralf Schumacher is over. Williams have promoted test driver Antônio Pizzonia into the race seat for the remainder of Schumacher's recovery; this is Pizzonia's first race for Williams.

### Practice

BAR third driver Anthony Davidson topped the first session, with Schumacher second and Räikkönen third; Schumacher and Räikkönen led the second. Schumacher was quickest in the third session, ahead of Montoya and Barrichello; Button led the fourth ahead of Montoya.
