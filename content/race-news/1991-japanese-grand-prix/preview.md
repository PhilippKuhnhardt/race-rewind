### Championship standings

Senna leads Mansell by 16 points with two races remaining. This is the fifth consecutive year the title will be decided at Suzuka.

### Previous race

At the new Circuit de Catalunya, Mansell won with Senna fifth — the gap has narrowed. Senna let Berger through early in the race and then spun at the last corner, dropping from second to fifth; Schumacher challenged strongly before spinning while trying to pass Berger.

### Between-race developments

Leyton House have replaced Ivan Capelli with young Austrian Karl Wendlinger, who brings sponsorship to the team. AGS have run out of money and will not travel to Japan. Coloni have hired 1990 Japanese Formula 3 champion Naoki Hattori to replace Pedro Chaves. Johnny Herbert is back at Lotus. Éric Bernard broke his ankle during qualifying at the previous race and cannot take part.

### Championship permutations

Mansell must win with help, while Senna needs only to finish ahead of Mansell.
