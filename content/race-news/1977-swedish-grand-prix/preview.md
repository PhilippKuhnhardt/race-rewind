### Previous race

At Zolder, the race ran wet. Andretti ran into the back of Watson on lap 1 at the chicane while trying to retake the lead, ending both of their races and handing Scheckter the front. The track dried, tyre stops reshuffled the order, and Gunnar Nilsson drove away to his first career victory ahead of Lauda. Ronnie Peterson completed the podium for Tyrrell — the only Formula 1 race in which two Swedes have shared the podium.
