### Milestones

This race marks the final Grand Prix appearance for two veterans: José Froilán González, who scored Ferrari's first ever World Championship victory at the 1951 British Grand Prix, and Harry Schell. Venezuelan driver Ettore Chimeri makes his only Grand Prix start — he is the first Formula One driver from Venezuela. Tragically, Chimeri dies two weeks later in Havana.
