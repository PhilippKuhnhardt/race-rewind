### Previous race

At the Hungaroring, Boutsen won from Senna by 0.288 seconds, holding him off in the final laps despite fading brakes. Prost retired with a gearbox failure. On lap 64, Senna attempted to pass Nannini at the chicane and the two collided, putting Nannini out; on lap 72, Berger attempted the same move on Mansell at the same corner, putting both cars out.

### Championship standings

Senna leads Prost by ten points, 54 to 44. Berger is third with 29, Boutsen fourth with 27.

### Between-race developments

The Onyx team has withdrawn from Formula One for financial reasons ahead of this race. Their departure means Ligier, who had only just dropped into pre-qualifying, are now relieved of the requirement and return to the main pool.

Nigel Mansell is being strongly linked with a move to Williams for 1991, reportedly having demanded undisputed number-one status and assurances about the Renault engine — conditions Frank Williams has said are impossible to guarantee.
