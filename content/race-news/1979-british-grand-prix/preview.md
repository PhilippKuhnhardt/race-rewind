### Previous race

Jabouille won the French Grand Prix at Dijon for Renault — the team's first Formula One victory and the first ever win for a turbocharged engine. The closing laps featured a legendary wheel-to-wheel duel between Villeneuve and Arnoux over second place, the two touching wheels and swapping positions repeatedly across the final two laps. Villeneuve described it as "my best memory of Grand Prix racing." Jabouille was the first Frenchman to win the French Grand Prix since Jean-Pierre Wimille in 1948.
