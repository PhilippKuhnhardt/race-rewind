### Championship standings

Andretti leads the championship by a growing margin. Lauda retired in France and remains well off the pace. Peterson holds second in the standings.

### Previous race

Andretti won France — his third win in four races since Belgium — with Peterson again second for another Lotus 1–2. Watson had taken pole for Brabham but was passed by Andretti on lap one and fell back; Hunt completed the podium. Lauda suffered yet another engine failure, his fourth of the season.

### Entrants

Keke Rosberg replaces Eddie Cheever at Theodore. Cheever, whose F1 debut with the team was brief, has returned to Formula Two.

### Track changes

The British Grand Prix moves to Brands Hatch, which last hosted the race in 1976. Silverstone will return in 1979. With 31 entries — the largest of the season so far — the Martini of René Arnoux has been excluded from qualifying to reduce the grid to 30 cars.
