### Championship standings

Alain Prost leads the Drivers' Championship by three points over Ayrton Senna following Senna's win in Monaco.

### Previous race

At Monaco, Senna ran second behind Mansell — who led from pole — until Mansell retired on lap 30 with lost boost pressure, handing Senna the lead. It was the first Grand Prix victory for a car running active suspension. Prost was running third when his engine failed three laps from the end.

### Between-race developments

The Canadian Grand Prix, originally scheduled for 14 June, has been confirmed as cancelled following an unresolved sponsorship dispute between rival breweries Labatt and Molson. Detroit is the only North American round on the 1987 calendar — the first time there has been no Canadian race since 1975.

The entry is unchanged from Monaco.
