### Previous race

Ferrari dominated the season opener at Albert Park. Michael Schumacher led every lap from pole to take a lights-to-flag victory, with teammate Rubens Barrichello second — the team's first one-two since the 2002 Japanese Grand Prix. Fernando Alonso gave Renault a podium in third; the rest of the field finished more than 20 seconds behind the Ferraris.

### Penalties

Giorgio Pantano carries a ten-place grid penalty for using the spare car — equipped with a different engine — during the Australian Grand Prix weekend, the first driver to trigger this new rule. Fernando Alonso and Takuma Sato also carry ten-place penalties for engine changes. Pantano starts from the pit lane.
