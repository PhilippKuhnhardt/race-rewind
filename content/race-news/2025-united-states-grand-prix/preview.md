### Championship standings

Piastri leads the Drivers' Championship with 336 points, twenty-two ahead of Norris in second. Verstappen is third, sixty-three points behind Piastri. McLaren secured the Constructors' Championship at the preceding Singapore Grand Prix.

### Previous race

Russell won in Singapore from pole — McLaren clinched the Constructors' Championship at the same event, their second consecutive title and first back-to-back since 1991. It was the first Singapore Grand Prix to end with the crowning of a Constructors' Champion, and the first Singapore race to feature zero retirements.

### Sprint format

The United States Grand Prix is the fourth sprint weekend of the season.

### Weather

The FIA has declared a heat hazard at this event, with the heat index forecast to exceed 31 °C during both the sprint and the race.

### Tyre choices

Pirelli brings the C1, C3 and C4 compounds — designated hard, medium and soft.

### Practice

The sole free practice session was topped by Norris ahead of Hülkenberg and Piastri. Leclerc experienced a gearbox issue during the session.
