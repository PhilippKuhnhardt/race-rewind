### Previous race

Regazzoni won at Silverstone — the first Formula One victory for the Williams team — with Arnoux second and Jarier third. Jones led comfortably until his engine overheated on lap 39. Laffite and Villeneuve both retired with mechanical trouble in the closing stages. Scheckter was lapped by Regazzoni and fell to fifth.

### Championship standings

Scheckter still leads the championship. Williams, who won their first race at Silverstone two weeks ago, arrive at the ultra-fast Hockenheim circuit looking to build on their breakthrough.

### Entrants

Jean-Pierre Jarier is in hospital with a liver problem. Tyrrell has replaced him with Formula 2 driver Geoff Lees for the German Grand Prix. The rest of the entry is unchanged from Silverstone.

### Milestones

This is the 25th Grand Prix start for Arrows.
