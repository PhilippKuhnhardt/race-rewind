### Championship standings

Hamilton leads Rosberg by 9 points. Red Bull Racing holds second in the Constructors' Championship, Mercedes ahead by 181 points.

### Previous race

Rosberg won Belgium from his first-row start while Hamilton climbed from last place — the back of a 22-car grid — to third. A multi-car incident at the start involving Verstappen, Vettel and Räikkönen eliminated all three from contention. Kevin Magnussen crashed heavily at Raidillon, causing the race to be stopped to repair the damaged tyre barrier; he sustained a cut on his ankle but confirmed afterwards that he would race in Italy.

### Penalties

Romain Grosjean and Esteban Ocon each carry five-place grid penalties for unscheduled gearbox changes.
