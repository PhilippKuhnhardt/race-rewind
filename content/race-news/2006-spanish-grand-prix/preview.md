### Championship standings

Alonso leads on 44 points to Schumacher's 31. Räikkönen is third on 23. Fisichella holds fourth on 18, one place ahead of Massa on 15. In the Constructors' Championship, Renault lead on 62 points from Ferrari on 46, with McLaren third on 42.

### Previous race

Schumacher won his second race of the season at the Nürburgring, with Alonso second and Felipe Massa third — Massa's first career podium finish. Franck Montagny made his Formula One début for Super Aguri, promoted after Yuji Ide's superlicence was revoked. A red flag for a software glitch in the timing system briefly interrupted Q1, and Jacques Villeneuve had his three fastest Q3 times deleted for blocking Fisichella.

### Practice

Felipe Massa led the first Friday session. Honda's third driver Anthony Davidson topped the second Friday session. Michael Schumacher headed the Saturday morning session.

### Milestones

This is the 48th running of the Spanish Grand Prix at the Circuit de Catalunya.
