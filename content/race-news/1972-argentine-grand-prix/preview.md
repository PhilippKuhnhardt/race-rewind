### Entrants

The Buenos Aires circuit returns to the Formula One calendar for the first time since 1960. Carlos Reutemann makes his World Championship debut for Brabham — the first Argentine driver to race at home in the championship — and qualifies on pole position. Reigning champion Jackie Stewart starts from second; Peter Revson lines up third in his first race for McLaren. BRM fields five cars at the opening round.

### Milestones

Reutemann takes pole position on his World Championship debut.
