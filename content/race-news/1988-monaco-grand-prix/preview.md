### Championship standings

McLaren has won both opening rounds of the season. The team's Honda turbo advantage is proving decisive even at this stage of the calendar. Monaco — a circuit historically kind to turbocharged cars on its short, twisting layout — is unlikely to change that picture.

### Entrants

Satoru Nakajima's Lotus-Honda failed to pre-qualify, a striking result given that Lotus runs the same Honda turbo engine as race-leading McLaren.
