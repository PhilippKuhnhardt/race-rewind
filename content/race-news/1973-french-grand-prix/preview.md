### Previous race

Hulme won the Swedish Grand Prix after Peterson's tyres failed with three laps remaining, with Peterson finishing second and Cevert third. Stewart salvaged two points from fifth after suffering rear brake failure in the closing stages.

### Entrants

Ensign make their Formula One debut with 1972 British Formula Three champion Rikky von Opel, who commissioned the team's first chassis. Jody Scheckter joins Denny Hulme at McLaren for a one-off factory drive, in only his third Formula One race.

### Circuit change

The French Grand Prix moves from Circuit de Charade to Circuit Paul Ricard for the first time, featuring two twisty sections linked by the 1.8 km Mistral Straight.
