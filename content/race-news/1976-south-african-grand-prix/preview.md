### Championship standings

Lauda leads the championship after Brazil. Hunt and Depailler share second place, both pointless or minimal — Lauda's win while Hunt retired means the gap is already notable after just one round.

### Previous race

Lauda won the Brazilian Grand Prix from lights to flag after Regazzoni led early. Hunt retired with a sticking throttle; Jarier, who had run second, also dropped out after driving through oil Hunt had laid on the circuit. Depailler finished second in the Tyrrell and Pryce third in the Shadow.

### Between-race developments

Ronnie Peterson has left Lotus after just one race, joining the March team. Lella Lombardi takes his seat at Lotus. Gunnar Nilsson, originally signed to March, has been loaned to Lotus in exchange.

### Milestones

This is the Formula One World Championship debut for Gunnar Nilsson. It is also the 100th pole position by a British driver in World Championship racing.
