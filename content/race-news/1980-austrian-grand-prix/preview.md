### Previous race

Germany went to Laffite, with Reutemann second and Jones third. Jones led until a puncture forced a pit stop; both Renaults failed with engine problems within a lap of each other. Jones extended his championship lead over Piquet to seven points. Reutemann climbed to third in the standings.

### Championship

Jones leads Piquet by seven points. Reutemann is third, 15 points behind Jones; Laffite is fourth, 16 behind.

### Entrants

Team Lotus run a third car for British driver Nigel Mansell, making his Formula One debut. Jochen Mass is unable to start after crashing and rolling his Arrows in practice, injuring himself.

### Background

The Österreichring is the fastest circuit of the season, set in the mountains of Styria. The high-altitude layout is expected to favour the turbocharged Renaults in qualifying, though Jones has shown he can compete with them at Hockenheim.
