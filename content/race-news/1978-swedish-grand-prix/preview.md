### Championship standings

Andretti has opened a lead at the top of the championship. Peterson has closed the gap to the leaders after back-to-back second places in Belgium and Spain.

### Previous race

Andretti won Spain for a second consecutive Lotus 1–2, Peterson again runner-up, Laffite third for Ligier. Reutemann had a massive accident on lap 58 when his Ferrari launched over a kerb, flew into the air, cleared the barrier and landed in the debris fencing; he escaped unhurt.

### Between-race developments

Brabham have introduced the BT46B for this race. The car features a large rear fan that draws air through horizontally-mounted radiators, while also creating suction beneath the car to generate ground effect — a direct counter to the Lotus 79's sliding-skirt concept developed by Colin Chapman. The car's legality is disputed: the rules prohibit moveable aerodynamic devices, but Brabham argue that the fan's primary function is engine cooling, not aerodynamics. Following protests, the stewards have allowed it to race.

### Milestones

This is the last Formula One Swedish Grand Prix to be held, as of the start of this season.
