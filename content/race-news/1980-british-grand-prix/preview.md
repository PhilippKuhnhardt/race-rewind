### Previous race

France went to Jones, who won by four seconds from Pironi after working past the Ligiers of Laffite and then Pironi over the second half of the race. Laffite had led for much of the race until his tyres went off. Jones now leads the championship for the first time since South Africa.

### Championship

Jones leads the championship. This round opens the second half of the season: only the best five results from rounds 8–14 will count toward the Drivers' Championship.

### Between-race developments

Brabham have replaced Ricardo Zunino with Mexican driver Héctor Rebaque for the rest of the season. Shadow Racing Cars, having failed to qualify at the previous round in France, have closed down.

### Entrants

RAM Racing has entered a pair of year-old Williams FW07s for Rupert Keegan and South African driver Desiré Wilson — the fourth woman to enter a Formula One World Championship event, after Maria Teresa de Filippis, Lella Lombardi, and Divina Galica.
