### Championship standings

Clark leads the championship after his win at Zandvoort on the Lotus 49's debut. Hulme and Rodríguez remain close behind.

### Previous race

Clark won convincingly at Zandvoort despite starting from eighth after driving the Lotus 49 for the first time in his life during practice. He fought through the field, passed Brabham for the lead on lap 16, and pulled away at over a second a lap. Brabham and Hulme finished second and third for Brabham Racing; the three Ferraris filled the next positions.
