### Championship standings

Häkkinen leads after winning Spain — his first win since Brazil — with Coulthard second and Schumacher third finishing the Spanish podium. The McLarens completed a 1-2 for the first time this season. Schumacher remains the championship leader on 30 points, but Häkkinen has moved to within striking distance.

### Previous race

At Barcelona, both McLarens got away cleanly while the Ferraris were caught behind Villeneuve's BAR in the early laps. The McLarens built a large enough advantage to cruise home — the first time both finished a race in 1999. Schumacher recovered to third at the pit stops. Villeneuve's race ended with a broken rear wing element and a failed gearbox in the pits.
