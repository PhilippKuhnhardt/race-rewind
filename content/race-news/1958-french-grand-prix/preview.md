### Previous race

At Spa, Tony Brooks won for Vanwall, twenty seconds ahead of Hawthorn. The drama came on the final lap: all three Vanwalls of Brooks, Hawthorn — running second in a Ferrari — and Lewis-Evans broke down exiting La Source, but each managed to coast across the finish line. Had the race been one lap longer, the result would have been very different. Cliff Allison gave Lotus their first championship points in fourth.

### Circuit

The French Grand Prix returns to Reims-Gueux after one year at Rouen-Les-Essarts.

### Entrants

This is the debut World Championship race for Phil Hill, who drives for Scuderia Ferrari. Five-time World Champion Juan Manuel Fangio makes his return to the championship after skipping Monaco and the Dutch GP. American Troy Ruttman enters a Maserati 250F for Scuderia Centro Sud.

### Practice

Ferrari's Hawthorn and Musso qualify on the front row. Harry Schell (BRM) lines up third.
