### Championship standings

Michael Schumacher leads with 66 points, eight ahead of Räikkönen in second. Ralf Schumacher is third on 55 points. Ferrari lead the Constructors' on 118 points, Williams second on 108, McLaren third on 95.

### Previous race

At Magny-Cours, Williams locked out the front row and the top two finishing positions. Ralf Schumacher won from pole for the second race running. Montoya was second and Michael Schumacher third. Both Renaults retired with engine failures in the second half of the race. Coulthard lost time when the refuelling hose remained attached to his car as he left his pit box.
