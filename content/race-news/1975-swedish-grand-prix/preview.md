### Championship standings

Lauda leads the championship for the first time in 1975 after winning Belgium, passing Fittipaldi who had held the lead since the opening round.

### Previous race

At Zolder, Lauda overtook Brambilla for the lead on lap six and controlled the race from there. Scheckter fought through the field to finish second, Reutemann third. Fittipaldi suffered brake problems late on, dropping from fifth to seventh. Tony Brise, in only his second race, took fourth on the grid and ran in the points before brake fade, earning Graham Hill's first championship point as a constructor.
