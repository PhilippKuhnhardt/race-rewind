### Championship standings

Stewart leads with four wins from five races, making this the first half of the season where the title picture is already becoming clear.

### Previous race

Stewart won the French Grand Prix at Charade. Beltoise gave the home crowd a second-place finish in what was only a thirteen-car race. Ickx completed the podium for Brabham. Miles' four-wheel-drive Lotus 63 debut lasted one lap before a fuel pump failure.

### Practice

McLaren bring their four-wheel-drive M7A-1 to Silverstone for the first time. Lotus have two four-wheel-drive cars and convince Hill to try one. Stewart also tests the four-wheel-drive Matra MS84 in practice, but crashes it heavily on a loose kerb going through the final corner; the car is irreparable before the race. Under the regulations, grid positions are based on the fastest time set in the car a driver actually starts — Stewart takes over his teammate's car and his fastest time in that car is used to set his grid slot.

### Entrants

Cosworth entered a car of their own for this race but withdrew it several weeks before the weekend.
