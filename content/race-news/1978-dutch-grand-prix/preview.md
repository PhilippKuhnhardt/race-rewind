### Previous race

Peterson won the Austrian Grand Prix — his first win since South Africa — in wet conditions. Andretti collided with Reutemann at the start and retired immediately. Peterson led from the off, surviving a shower that caught out multiple drivers. Depailler finished second, Villeneuve third. Several others crashed out in the rain.
