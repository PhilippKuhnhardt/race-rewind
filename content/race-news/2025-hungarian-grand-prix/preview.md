### Championship standings

Piastri leads the Drivers' Championship with 266 points, sixteen ahead of Norris and sixty-five ahead of Verstappen in third.

### Previous race

Piastri won the Belgian Grand Prix — the sprint was taken by Verstappen. Piastri and Norris completed McLaren's sixth one-two finish of the season, their most in a single season since Senna and Prost achieved ten in 1988. Norris suffered a late lock-up at La Source on the penultimate lap that ended any realistic chance of challenging for the lead.

### Entrants

Paul Aron replaces Hülkenberg at Sauber in FP1. Fernando Alonso sits out FP1 for Aston Martin with a muscular back injury, replaced by Felipe Drugovich.

### Tyre choices

Pirelli brings the C3, C4 and C5 compounds — designated hard, medium and soft.

### Practice

Norris topped both FP1 and FP2 ahead of Piastri and Leclerc. FP3 reversed the order slightly: Piastri, Norris and Leclerc completed the top three.
