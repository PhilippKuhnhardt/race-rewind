### Previous race

Collins won at Spa-Francorchamps, with Ferrari teammate Paul Frère — racing before his home crowd — finishing second for the first Formula One podium by a Belgian driver. Moss was third. Fangio retired without scoring for the first time since the 1953 Belgian Grand Prix, ending a streak of 21 consecutive points-scoring finishes.

### Entrants

Alfonso de Portago makes his championship debut, joining Scuderia Ferrari.

Bugatti makes a one-off appearance at Reims with their Type 251 — a rear mid-engined design driven by Maurice Trintignant.

Colin Chapman, founder of Team Lotus, makes his only Formula One World Championship entry with Vanwall, but crashes into his team leader during practice and does not start.

### Practice

Fangio takes his fourth consecutive pole position of the season, with teammates Castellotti and Collins making it an all-Ferrari front row.
