### Previous race

Schumacher led a Ferrari 1-2 at the Nürburgring with Barrichello second and Button third. Ralf Schumacher and Cristiano da Matta retired on the first lap after a collision triggered by Montoya. Sato took a front-row start but retired late with a gearbox failure; both McLarens also retired with engine failures.

### Between-race developments

Giorgio Pantano has been replaced at Jordan by Timo Glock, who makes his Formula One debut here. The change is financial; Pantano's departure leaves Jordan with an all-new driver pairing of Heidfeld and Glock.

### Milestones

The Canadian Grand Prix was originally dropped from the 2004 calendar over tobacco sponsorship complications before being reinstated.
