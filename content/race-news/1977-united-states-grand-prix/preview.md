### Championship standings

Lauda leads with 69 points. Scheckter is second with 42 points. With nine points for a win and three races remaining, Lauda needs only a single championship point in any of them to clinch the title.

### Championship permutations

Scheckter must win all three remaining races to have a chance. If he does, both drivers would finish on 69 points — but Scheckter would hold the tiebreaker on wins, with five to Lauda's three. Lauda needs only to score once.
