### Championship standings

Prost leads Lauda by 4.5 points after his German Grand Prix win. Prost and Lauda secured a McLaren 1–2 at Hockenheim, extending Prost's advantage to its widest point since the early part of the season.

### Previous race

Prost won at Hockenheim from pole for McLaren's second 1–2 of the season, with Lauda second and Warwick third. Senna retired when his rear wing failed and he spun into the wall near the Ostkurve chicane while running sixth.

### Entrants

Gerhard Berger makes his Formula One debut, driving an ATS-BMW.

### Milestones

This is the 400th Formula One World Championship Grand Prix since the series began in 1950. The Österreichring is the fastest circuit of the season with Silverstone absent from this year's calendar.
