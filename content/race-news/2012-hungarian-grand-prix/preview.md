### Championship standings

Alonso leads the championship on 154 points after Germany. Webber is second on 120, Vettel third on 110, Räikkönen fourth on 98, and Hamilton fifth on 92.

### Previous race

Alonso won in Germany ahead of Button and Räikkönen. It was Alonso's fourth win of the season and his second in a row. Vettel finished in the points but Hamilton and Webber struggled, leaving Alonso's lead growing to 34 points.

### Between-race developments

After Red Bull were found to have manipulated engine throttle mapping at Hockenheim to enhance exhaust-blown diffuser downforce — technically within the existing rules — the FIA rewrote the throttle-mapping regulations for Hungary, requiring teams to submit a "reference map" from an early race and restricting subsequent torque output changes. Red Bull were cleared for the German GP itself but are now subject to the new rules.

### Entrants

Clos replaces Karthikeyan at HRT for FP1. Bianchi drives for Force India (replacing Hülkenberg) and Bottas takes Senna's Williams seat in the first practice session.

### Tyre choices

Pirelli brings the white-banded medium compound as the prime and yellow-banded soft as the option. This is the first Hungarian Grand Prix in the single-supplier tyre era where the super-soft compound is not used.

### Weather

Fine, dry, and very hot conditions are forecast — 30°C air temperature, 45°C track.
