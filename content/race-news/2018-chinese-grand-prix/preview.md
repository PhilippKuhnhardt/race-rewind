### Championship standings

Sebastian Vettel leads Lewis Hamilton by seventeen points in the Drivers' Championship after winning both opening rounds. Ferrari lead Mercedes by ten points in the Constructors'.

### Previous race

Vettel won from pole in Bahrain, holding off a late charge from Bottas to take victory by 0.699 seconds. Hamilton finished third despite a five-place grid penalty for a gearbox change and contact with Verstappen on the second lap.

### Practice

Hamilton was fastest in FP1 and FP2. Vettel led FP3. During FP3, Ricciardo's turbo failed and required a full rebuild.
