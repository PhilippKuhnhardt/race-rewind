### Previous race

At Hockenheim, Lauda passed Scheckter in the early laps and pulled away for a comfortable win. Scheckter was second and Stuck third for Brabham. Watson led until his engine failed on lap 8, and Hunt retired later with a broken fuel pump.

### Track changes

The Österreichring has been modified since last year's Austrian Grand Prix. The ultra-fast Voest-Hugel Kurve has been replaced by a chicane, increasing the circuit length to 5.942 kilometres.
