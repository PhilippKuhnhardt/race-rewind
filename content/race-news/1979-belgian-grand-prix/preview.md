### Previous race

Depailler won in Spain with Reutemann second and Andretti third. Laffite's engine blew while challenging for the lead, while Scheckter slipped to fourth.

### Championship standings

The title picture remains close as the field arrives at Zolder two weeks after Spain.

### Entrants

Alfa Romeo returns to Formula One as a works constructor for the first time since 1951, entering Bruno Giacomelli in the new 177. This is the old flat-12 engine; a new chassis for the V12 is under development. The field grows to 28 cars with Alfa's arrival. Tyrrell arrives with new sponsorship from Italian domestic appliance maker Candy. McLaren's troubles continue: Watson destroyed the M28 in testing, and Tambay failed to qualify with an older M26.

### Milestones

This is the 50th Grand Prix start for a Dutch driver.
