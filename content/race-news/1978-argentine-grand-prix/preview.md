### Entrants

This is the first Formula One race for Williams Grand Prix Engineering using their own chassis, the FW06, driven by Alan Jones. Five constructors make their World Championship debut this season, though Arrows and Renault do not enter until the South African Grand Prix.

Divina Galica drives the Hesketh, and Eddie Cheever makes his F1 debut with Theodore.
