### Previous race

Lewis Hamilton won the Hungarian Grand Prix for Mercedes — his first win since joining the team — from Räikkönen and Vettel. Hamilton tied Michael Schumacher's record of four Hungarian Grand Prix victories, and became the first British driver to win in a Mercedes since Stirling Moss. Williams scored their first points of the season when Pastor Maldonado finished tenth after Nico Rosberg retired with an engine fire late in the race. Ferrari were fined €15,000 after Fernando Alonso activated DRS on three occasions when he was not within one second of another car, due to the team failing to reset the DRS enabling system from its pre-race setting.
