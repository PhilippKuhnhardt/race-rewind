### Previous race

At Brazil, Nelson Piquet won the season opener for Williams, overtaking pole-sitter Ayrton Senna on lap 3 and pulling away to win by 34 seconds. Nigel Mansell spun off on the opening lap after contact with Senna. Alain Prost looked set to challenge before his TAG-Porsche engine failed just past half-distance.

### Championship standings

Piquet leads the championship after winning the opening round.

### Circuit

This is the first Spanish Grand Prix since 1981 and the first Formula One race ever held at the newly built Circuito de Jerez.
