### Entrants

Frank Williams's Iso-Marlboro team makes its Formula One championship debut at Buenos Aires, one of several new entries joining the grid for the season opener.

### Milestones

Ferrari-powered cars line up for their 200th world championship race. In those 200 starts, Ferrari engines have powered 49 Grand Prix wins, 183 podium finishes, 60 pole positions, and six Drivers' titles.
