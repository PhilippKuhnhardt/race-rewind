### Championship standings

Russell leads the Drivers' Championship with 25 points, 7 ahead of teammate Antonelli and 10 ahead of Leclerc (Ferrari). Mercedes lead the Constructors' with 43 points; Ferrari are second with 27 and McLaren third with 10.

### Previous race

At Melbourne, Russell converted pole to a dominant win, leading teammate Antonelli home for a Mercedes 1–2. Leclerc and Hamilton completed the top four for Ferrari. Piastri did not start after crashing his McLaren during a reconnaissance lap following an unexpected power surge. Verstappen recovered from twentieth on the grid — where a qualifying crash had left him without a lap time — to finish sixth.

### Sprint format

Shanghai hosts the first sprint weekend of the 2026 season, and its third sprint event overall. Sprint qualifying replaces the second practice session; the sprint race follows on Saturday morning ahead of main qualifying.

### Milestones
