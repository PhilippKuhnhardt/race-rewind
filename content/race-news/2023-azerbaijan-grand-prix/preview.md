### Championship standings

Verstappen leads with 69 points, 15 ahead of Pérez and 24 ahead of Alonso in third. Red Bull lead the Constructors' Championship with 123 points; Aston Martin are second with 65, Mercedes third with 56.

### Previous race

At Melbourne, Verstappen recovered from wheelspin off the line — allowing Hamilton and Russell through — to retake the lead by lap 12 and win ahead of Hamilton and Alonso. The race set a new record for red flags with three: the first after Leclerc's crash with Stroll, the second after Albon's heavy impact, and a third following a multi-car collision at the final restart. Russell retired with a power unit failure on lap 18 after debris lodged in a cylinder.

### Sprint format

This is the first sprint weekend of the season, and the first under the new format. Friday features a single free practice session followed by Grand Prix qualifying. Saturday opens with the "sprint shootout" — three knockout segments of 12, 10, and 8 minutes — to set the sprint grid, with medium tyres mandatory in SQ1 and SQ2, and softs in SQ3. The sprint is held Saturday afternoon, with the Grand Prix on Sunday.
