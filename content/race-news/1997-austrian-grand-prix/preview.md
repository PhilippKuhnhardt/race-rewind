### Championship standings

The title fight remains between Schumacher and Villeneuve, with Coulthard and Frentzen contesting third place.

### Previous race

Coulthard won Italy from sixth on the grid — the only race of the season without either Villeneuve or Schumacher on the podium. Alesi finished second, Frentzen third. Neither Ferrari nor Williams started from the front row.

### Track changes

The Austrian Grand Prix returns to the calendar for the first time since 1987. The race is held at the A1-Ring, a shortened and redeveloped layout built on the site of the old Österreichring.

### Milestones

Gerhard Berger announces his retirement at the end of the season during the Austrian race weekend, having qualified 18th on the grid.
