### Championship standings

Max Verstappen is the 2023 World Drivers' Champion, having clinched the title during the Qatar sprint. Red Bull Racing secured the Constructors' Championship at the Japanese Grand Prix. With both titles decided, attention turns to the fight for second in the Constructors' between Mercedes and Ferrari, and the battle for second in the Drivers' between Pérez and Hamilton.

### Previous race

At Mexico City, Verstappen took his record-breaking 16th win of the season, ahead of Hamilton and Leclerc. Pérez collided with Leclerc at the first corner on lap one and retired with terminal damage. Magnussen had a heavy crash at turn 9 on lap 33, bringing out first a safety car and then a red flag to repair the barrier. The restart ran the remaining laps without further incident.

### Sprint format

This is the sixth and final sprint weekend of the 2023 season.
