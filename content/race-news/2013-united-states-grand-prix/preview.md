### Previous race

Sebastian Vettel won the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix from Webber, with Nico Rosberg third. Vettel's seventh consecutive victory equalled the Formula One record for consecutive wins held by Alberto Ascari and Michael Schumacher.

### Between-race developments

Kimi Räikkönen has announced he will miss the final two races of the season to undergo back surgery. He had suffered increasing discomfort over previous races from an injury he had wanted to delay treating until the off-season. His final race for Lotus was therefore Abu Dhabi, having agreed to join Ferrari for the 2014 season. Heikki Kovalainen has signed a two-race deal with Lotus to replace him.

### Penalties

Jenson Button receives a three-place grid penalty after overtaking under red flags during the opening practice session. Charles Pic receives a five-place grid penalty after Caterham changed the gearbox in his car.

### Tyre choices

Pirelli brings the hard (prime) and medium (option) compounds, as at the 2012 United States Grand Prix.
