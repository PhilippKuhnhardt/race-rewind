### Championship standings

Schumacher leads with 26 points after winning Monaco for the fourth time in his career. Irvine is second on 18, Häkkinen third on 14 — twelve points behind Schumacher. Frentzen is fourth on 13. Ferrari lead the Constructors' with 44 points, McLaren second on 20, Jordan third on 16.

### Previous race

Schumacher won Monaco from second on the grid, beating Häkkinen off the line and pulling clear. Coulthard retired from third with a mechanical failure. Häkkinen made an error at Mirabeau after a blown engine from Takagi's car left oil on track and fell to third, where he finished behind the two Ferraris.
