### Championship standings

Senna leads the championship after winning the Phoenix opener.

### Previous race

At Phoenix, Senna led every lap from pole and won his 27th Formula 1 race, with Prost second and Piquet third. Jordan and Häkkinen made their debuts. Gearbox failures eliminated Mansell, Patrese, and Alesi, while a collision between the stricken Patrese and Moreno ended both their afternoons.
