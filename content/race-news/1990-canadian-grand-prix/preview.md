### Previous race

At Monaco, Senna led every lap of his third win at the principality. Alain Prost retired on lap 30 with battery failure — his third retirement in four races — while Mansell retired with the same problem late on after charging through the field. Nelson Piquet received a push start from marshals after stalling and was disqualified. Alesi took second again, his second consecutive podium.
