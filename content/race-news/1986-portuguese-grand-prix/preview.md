### Previous race

At Monza, Piquet took his fourth win of the season, passing Mansell at the Curva Grande to win by 9.8 seconds. Senna's gearbox failed at the start. Prost was disqualified after the formation lap for switching to the spare McLaren — an illegal move — and his engine blew shortly after anyway. Johansson finished third. The win moved Piquet into second in the championship, five points behind Mansell and three ahead of Prost, with three races remaining.

### Championship standings

Mansell leads, with Piquet five points behind and Prost eight back. Three races remain.

### Entrants

Allen Berg returns to the Osella entry after his one-race absence at Monza, where Alex Caffi had deputised. FISA again permits all 27 entered cars to start.
