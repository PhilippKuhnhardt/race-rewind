### Previous race

Emerson Fittipaldi won at Watkins Glen in only his fourth Grand Prix start — the first victory for a Brazilian driver in Formula One. Pedro Rodríguez finished second, Reine Wisell third on his debut. Ickx could manage only fourth. As a result, Jochen Rindt is confirmed as the posthumous World Drivers' Champion, with Lotus also having clinched the Constructors' title.

### Milestones

Three-time World Champion Jack Brabham makes his final Formula One start this weekend. He lines up fourth on the grid alongside the "three Jacks" — Stewart second, Ickx third. A crowd of around 200,000 is expected at the Magdalena Mixhuca circuit.

### Qualifying

Clay Regazzoni is on pole position for Ferrari, ahead of Stewart, Ickx, and Brabham.
