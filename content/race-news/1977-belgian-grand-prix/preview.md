### Previous race

At Monaco, Watson took the first pole position of his career in the Brabham. Scheckter led from the first corner, with Watson second until a gearbox failure mid-race allowed Lauda to close in. Scheckter held firm for his second win of the season. Lauda was second and Reutemann third.

### Championship standings

Scheckter leads the championship with two wins in six races. Lauda, back to full fitness after missing Spain, returns to form at the back of a season that has been unusually competitive across several teams.
