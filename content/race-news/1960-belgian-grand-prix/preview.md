### Previous race

Brabham won at Zandvoort ahead of Moss and Ireland. Brabham's car threw up a kerb that hit Moss, causing a puncture and a pit stop that cost him significant time.

### Practice

Stirling Moss suffered an axle failure at the high-speed Burnenville corner during practice, breaking both legs. He will not race this weekend. Mike Taylor experienced a steering failure at Stavelot during practice; his car hit the trees and his injuries ended his racing career.
