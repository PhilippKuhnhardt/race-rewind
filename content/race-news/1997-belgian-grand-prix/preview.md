### Championship standings

Villeneuve has closed the gap to Schumacher after Hungary, narrowing what had been a ten-point deficit.

### Previous race

Villeneuve won the Hungarian Grand Prix on the final lap, passing a slowing Damon Hill just as Hill reported hydraulic problems. Hill had led for most of the race after qualifying third — his strongest performance all season in the Arrows — and was set to give the team their first-ever victory before the failure. Herbert finished third. It was Williams' 100th Grand Prix victory.

### Entrants

Ralf Schumacher crashed at Stavelot while driving to his grid position, and is forced to start from the pit lane in his spare car.
