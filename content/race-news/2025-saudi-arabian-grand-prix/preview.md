### Championship standings

Norris leads on 77 points, three ahead of Piastri and eight ahead of Verstappen in third. McLaren lead the Constructors' with 151 points, 58 clear of Mercedes.

### Previous race

Piastri won in Bahrain to record his and McLaren's first victory at the circuit. Russell finished second but was briefly under investigation for a suspected DRS infringement. Verstappen suffered two slow pit stops and dropped to sixth. Gasly scored Alpine's first points of the season in seventh, and Haas scored a double points finish.

### Tyre choices

Pirelli brings the C3, C4 and C5 compounds — designated hard, medium and soft.

### Practice

Gasly topped FP1 ahead of Norris and Leclerc. Tsunoda crashed heavily at turn 27 in the closing stages of FP2, causing a red flag; the session had been led by Norris ahead of Piastri and Verstappen. Norris then topped FP3 with Piastri second and Russell third.
