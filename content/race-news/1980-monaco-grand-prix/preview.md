### Previous race

Belgium went to Pironi for his first career win, with Jones second and Reutemann third. Arnoux, who arrived level with Piquet at the top, collected three points in fourth — leaving Jones just two points behind the championship leader.

### Championship

Arnoux leads the championship by two points from Jones, with Piquet one point further back and Pironi one behind him.

### Background

Monaco uses a shortened grid of just 20 cars, meaning seven drivers from the full entry list will be eliminated in qualifying. John Watson has already failed to qualify here.

### Milestones

This is the 38th Monaco Grand Prix and the 100th World Championship start for an Alfa Romeo-powered car.
