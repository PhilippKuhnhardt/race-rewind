### Previous race

At Paul Ricard, Prost won his third race in succession — Ferrari's 100th victory in Formula One — closing the gap to Senna to just three points. The afternoon's real story came from Leyton House: Capelli and Gugelmin ran first and second for nearly two-thirds of the race before Prost caught Capelli in the closing laps and Gugelmin's engine blew. It was the team's first podium.

### Championship standings

Prost trails Senna by just three points at the halfway point of the season.

### Milestones

Riccardo Patrese starts his 200th Grand Prix, becoming the first driver in history to reach the milestone.

### Circuit

Silverstone is the fastest circuit on the calendar, with Mansell qualifying at an average speed of 158 mph. This is the last race on the original high-speed layout — construction crews begin reprofiling and adding new corners immediately after the race, funded by Tom Walkinshaw.

### Qualifying

Mansell took pole by more than half a second from Senna, roared on by his home crowd. The Life team had no pit garage and prepared their car on the grass beside the pitlane.

### Between-race developments

Subaru ended their involvement with the Coloni team after eight consecutive failures to pre-qualify. Coloni will source new engines for the next race in Germany.
