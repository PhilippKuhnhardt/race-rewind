### Previous race

At Porto, Moss won for Vanwall on the street circuit while Hawthorn finished second after a dramatic late episode in which he spun and stalled his car, then push-started it without assistance to reclaim the position. Lewis-Evans was third.

### Between-race developments

After the race, Hawthorn faced a post-race stewards' inquiry over the push-start incident. Moss, despite having a personal interest in the outcome, appeared before the judges to argue that Hawthorn had done nothing wrong. The decision was ultimately reversed and Hawthorn's second place reinstated.

### Entrants

American Carroll Shelby and Masten Gregory are entered to share a car for the race. Under the current regulations, no championship points are awarded for a shared drive.
