### Championship standings

Lewis Hamilton leads Sebastian Vettel by seventy points in the Drivers' Championship. Räikkönen is third, fifty-five points further back. Mercedes lead Ferrari by sixty-six points in the Constructors', with Red Bull a further one hundred and sixty points behind in third.

### Previous race

Räikkönen won the United States Grand Prix for his first victory since the 2013 Australian Grand Prix, ending a 114-race winless run. Verstappen finished second, Hamilton third, Vettel fourth. Hamilton needed to outscore Vettel by eight points to clinch the title in Austin but fell three short.

### Championship permutations

For the championship to remain alive beyond Mexico, Vettel must win and Hamilton must finish eighth or lower.
