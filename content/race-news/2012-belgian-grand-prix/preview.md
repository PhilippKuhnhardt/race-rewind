### Championship standings

Alonso leads the championship on 164 points after Hungary. Webber is second on 124, Vettel third on 122, Hamilton fourth on 117, and Räikkönen fifth on 116 — four drivers within 48 points of second place.

### Previous race

Hamilton won in Hungary from Räikkönen and Grosjean, with Alonso fourth. The result trimmed Alonso's lead to 40 points but left Vettel outside the top two for a second consecutive race.

### Milestones

This is Michael Schumacher's 300th Formula One race start.

### Tyre choices

Pirelli brings the silver-banded hard compound as the prime and white-banded medium as the option.

### Practice

FP1 was wet. Kobayashi ran early and set the fastest time. Massa's Ferrari failed to set a lap when the car stalled near the pits.

FP2 ran in even heavier rain with standing water. Only a handful of drivers set competitive lap times; Pic (Marussia) was fastest of those who ventured out.

FP3 ran dry. Alonso topped the session.

### Weather

Fine and dry conditions are forecast for race day — 23°C air temperature, 33°C track.
