### Championship standings

Lewis Hamilton leads Sebastian Vettel by fourteen points in the Drivers' Championship. Mercedes lead Ferrari by twenty-three points in the Constructors'.

### Previous race

Hamilton won the returning French Grand Prix, taking the championship lead back with fourteen points over Vettel. Verstappen finished second after Vettel and Bottas collided at the first corner, retiring both. Räikkönen completed the podium.

### Track notes

The Red Bull Ring features three DRS zones for the first time: one along the main straight, a second on the straight from turn 1 to turns 2–3, and a third extending from turn 3 to turn 4.
