### Championship standings

Stewart leads the championship. Ickx won at Zandvoort to remain his closest rival; Rodríguez has been scoring consistently for BRM.

### Previous race

Netherlands: Heavy rain at Zandvoort produced a commanding win for Ickx ahead of Rodríguez — the first race in 42 consecutive rounds without a Ford-Cosworth-powered car on the podium. Andretti's Ferrari missed the start with a fuel pump problem and retired within a few laps.

### Track changes

This is the first French Grand Prix held at Circuit Paul Ricard. The previous two runnings were at the twisty Charade public-road circuit near Clermont-Ferrand. Paul Ricard, near Marseille, is one of the most modern circuits in the world, featuring a smooth surface, state-of-the-art facilities, and a 1.1-mile straight along the Mistral section.

### Car upgrades

Tyrrell bring new front bodywork for Stewart's car, developed after extensive tyre testing in the weeks prior to the race.

### Entrants

French drivers Max Jean and François Mazet make their Formula One debuts on home ground. Emerson Fittipaldi is racing while in pain, heavily bandaged after a road accident.
