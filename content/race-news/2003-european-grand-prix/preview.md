### Championship standings

Michael Schumacher leads the Drivers' Championship with 54 points, three clear of Räikkönen on 51. A group of four drivers — Alonso (34), Ralf Schumacher (33), Barrichello (32), and Montoya (31) — contest third place. Ferrari lead the Constructors' on 86 points, nine clear of McLaren; Williams are third.

### Previous race

In Canada, Michael Schumacher won while nursing an ailing car across the final laps, holding off a closing Montoya and Alonso. Schumacher took the lead in the championship for the first time in the season, ahead of Räikkönen by three points. Räikkönen failed to set a time in qualifying after spinning off at the second corner, starting from the pit lane. Just nine drivers finished the race. Barrichello hit Alonso at the start; Pizzonia ran into Trulli.
