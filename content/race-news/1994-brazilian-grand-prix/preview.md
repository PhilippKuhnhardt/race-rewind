### Entrants

Five drivers make their Formula One debuts this weekend: Heinz-Harald Frentzen at Sauber, Olivier Panis at Ligier, Jos Verstappen standing in for the injured JJ Lehto at Benetton, Olivier Beretta at Larrousse, and Roland Ratzenberger at Simtek. Two new constructors also take to the grid for the first time — Simtek, who previously contributed to the Andrea Moda S921 design, and Pacific Racing. Brazil is the first Grand Prix since 1983 to permit refuelling during pit stops.
