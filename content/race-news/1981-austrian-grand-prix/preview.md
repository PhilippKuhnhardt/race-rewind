### Championship standings

Piquet's third win of the season at Hockenheim moves him to within eight points of championship leader Reutemann, who retired with an engine failure.

### Previous race

At Hockenheim, Piquet inherited victory when Jones, who had fought past Prost for the lead, suffered a misfiring engine and pitted in the closing laps as rain fell. Prost finished second, Laffite third. Reutemann retired on lap 28 with an engine failure; Arnoux was also delayed by a puncture caused by contact with Piquet in the opening moments.

### Between-race developments

The Fittipaldi team is absent, unable to secure enough engines. Tyrrell has switched back from Avon to Goodyear. Eddie Cheever fails to qualify in the new Tyrrell 011.

### Circuit notes

The Österreichring's extra altitude gives a natural advantage to turbocharged cars. The Renaults of Prost and Arnoux and Villeneuve's Ferrari are expected to benefit from the thin air.
