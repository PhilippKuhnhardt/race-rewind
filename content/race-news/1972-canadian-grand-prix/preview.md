### Championship standings

Emerson Fittipaldi is the 1972 World Champion, having clinched the title at Monza. At 25 years and 273 days, he is the youngest Formula One World Champion in history.

### Previous race

Fittipaldi won at Monza from sixth on the grid after a chaotic race saw Stewart retire seconds after the start with engine failure, Regazzoni and Ickx both retire late, and much of the field thin out. Hailwood finished second for Surtees; Hulme third.

### Entrants

Mont Tremblant has been closed following a dispute with local racing authorities; Mosport Park becomes the sole host of the Canadian Grand Prix and has been upgraded to meet modern racing standards. McLaren's drivers are familiar with Mosport from their Can-Am programme. Revson qualifies on pole, Hulme second, Peterson third.

### Milestones

Revson's pole position is the first for McLaren in a World Championship race.
