### Championship standings

Hamilton has a chance to win the World Drivers' Championship at this race if he outscores his only remaining rival, Bottas, by 14 points or more.

### Previous race

Bottas won Japan from the front. Verstappen retired after first-lap contact with Leclerc — the stewards later handed Leclerc a five-second penalty for causing the collision and a further ten-second penalty for driving in an unsafe condition. Hamilton finished third, claiming the fastest lap bonus point, and with Mercedes outscoring Ferrari by 17 points the team secured their sixth consecutive Constructors' Championship. Renault were subsequently disqualified from the race results — both cars — for using an illegal driver aid following a protest from Racing Point.

### Entrants
