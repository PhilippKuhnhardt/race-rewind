### Championship standings

Going into the final round, Ginther leads the fight for runner-up on 29 points, ahead of Hill on 25 and Surtees on 22. All three can still finish second in the championship.

### Previous race

At Mexico City, Clark dominated from pole position to claim his sixth win of the season. John Surtees was disqualified after needing a push-start from his mechanics following his pit stop. Brabham inherited third and held it to the flag.

### Entrants

Six drivers make their Formula One World Championship debuts at East London: South Africans Paddy Driver, Peter de Klerk, Brausch Niemann, and Trevor Blokdyk; Rhodesian Sam Tingle; and British driver David Prophet.

### Milestones

A fastest lap here would be the first for the Brabham team in World Championship competition. This is the final Formula One race held in December — a distinction it will hold until the 2019 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix.
