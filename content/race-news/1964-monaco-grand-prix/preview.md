### Entrants

Peter Arundell makes his Formula One debut with Team Lotus, promoted from the team's Formula Junior programme to replace Trevor Taylor. Phil Hill, the 1961 World Champion, joins Cooper, replacing Tony Maggs following ATS's withdrawal from the sport. American driver Peter Revson also makes his World Championship debut this weekend.

Reigning champion Jim Clark opens his title defence having won seven of the ten races in 1963. Graham Hill lines up for BRM alongside teammate Richie Ginther, while Jack Brabham and Dan Gurney represent the works Brabham team.
