### Previous race

Brabham won at Reims ahead of Gendebien and McLaren, extending his championship lead.

### Entrants

John Surtees makes only his second Formula One Grand Prix start, again with Lotus. Lance Reventlow and Chuck Daigh share a single Scarab entry — the car carrying damage from France. Daigh was faster in practice and takes the drive. Aston Martin makes a rare appearance, entering Roy Salvadori and Maurice Trintignant in the DBR5 — their only World Championship outing of the season.

Stirling Moss remains absent, continuing his recovery from the injuries sustained at Spa.
