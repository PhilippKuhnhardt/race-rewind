### Championship standings

Max Verstappen leads the Drivers' Championship by 63 points from Charles Leclerc in second, with Sergio Pérez a further seven points behind in third after the French Grand Prix. Red Bull Racing leads Ferrari by 82 points and Mercedes by 126 in the Constructors' Championship.

### Previous race

Verstappen won France after Leclerc, who had led from pole, spun and hit the tyre barrier on lap 18 — a driver error. Hamilton finished second and Russell third. Pérez fourth, Sainz fifth.

### Entrants

Robert Kubica drives for Alfa Romeo in place of Valtteri Bottas during FP1.

### Tyre choices

Pirelli brings the C2, C3, and C4 compounds (hard, medium, soft).

### Practice

Sainz was fastest in FP1 ahead of Verstappen and Leclerc. Leclerc topped FP2 ahead of Norris and Sainz.

In FP3, Vettel crashed out at turn 10, bringing out a red flag. After the restart, Nicholas Latifi set the fastest time ahead of Leclerc and Albon.
