### Championship standings

Häkkinen leads with 40 points, eight ahead of Schumacher on 32. Irvine is third on 26 — still in contention with half the season to run.

### Previous race

Frentzen won France from fifth on the grid in a wet race that few expected him to lead. Häkkinen charged from 14th on the grid but spun mid-race and eventually recovered to finish second. Barrichello ran in the lead for extended periods before finishing third. Frentzen's win was his first of the season.

Hill, who hinted after France that it might have been his final race, is entered again at Silverstone. He had considered leaving the sport immediately but has not confirmed a decision either way.
