### Championship standings

Prost leads the championship. Lauda won in South Africa with Prost recovering to second from the pitlane, keeping the Frenchman ahead.

### Previous race

Lauda won at Kyalami after the Brabhams of Piquet and Fabi both retired with turbo failures. Prost started from the pitlane following a fuel pump failure on his race car, and carved through the field to second. Senna scored his first World Championship point, sixth on his second career start.

### Track changes

The Belgian Grand Prix returns to Zolder after last year's race at Spa-Francorchamps — the two venues alternate under an agreement between the Belgian organisers. This is the tenth time the Belgian Grand Prix has been held at Zolder.
