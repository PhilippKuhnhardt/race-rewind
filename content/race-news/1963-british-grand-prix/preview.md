### Championship standings

Clark leads the championship by a commanding margin. His three wins in three starts since Monaco have separated him from Hill and the BRM challenge.

### Previous race

At Reims, Hill stalled on the grid and was push-started, incurring a one-minute time penalty — and ultimately no championship points for his classified third-place finish. Clark won from start to finish again, with Tony Maggs of Cooper taking second. Brabham had been closing before an ignition lead came loose and he dropped back.

### Entrants

The British Grand Prix moves from Aintree to Silverstone — its first appearance there since 1960. Aintree and Silverstone had alternated as host since 1955; Aintree's 1962 race was its last in Formula One. This is the 18th British Grand Prix.

Mike Hailwood, Bob Anderson, and Ian Raby all make their Formula One World Championship debuts.
