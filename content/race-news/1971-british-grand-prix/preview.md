### Championship standings

Stewart leads by a widening margin after four wins from five rounds. Ickx is his nearest rival but has been unable to match Stewart's consistency.

### Previous race

France: Stewart and Cevert finished first and second for Tyrrell on the fast Paul Ricard circuit. Fittipaldi took third despite racing while recovering from a road accident. Rodríguez had run second before his BRM retired with ignition failure.

### Between-race developments

Pedro Rodríguez was killed on 11 July while driving a Ferrari 512 in the Interserie sports car race at the Norisring street circuit in Nuremberg, Germany. He was fourth in the Drivers' Championship at the time. BRM arrive at Silverstone in mourning.

### Entrants

Mike Beuttler makes his Formula One debut.

### Milestones

This is Tyrrell's 10th championship race as a constructor.
