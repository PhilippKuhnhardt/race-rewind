### Championship standings

After Monaco, Musso holds the championship lead. Moss and Trintignant are joint second, four points behind.

### Previous race

At Monaco, Maurice Trintignant won for Rob Walker Racing in the team's new Cooper T45, leading home the Ferraris of Musso and Collins after Hawthorn (fuel pump) and Moss (engine) both retired. Rob Walker Racing has now won the opening two races of the season in consecutive appearances.

### Between-race developments

Fangio, absent from Monaco while preparing for the Indianapolis 500, also skips Zandvoort.

### Practice

Vanwall have locked out the entire front row: Stuart Lewis-Evans, Moss, and Brooks hold the top three grid positions. Harry Schell starts seventh in the BRM but advances quickly in the early laps.
