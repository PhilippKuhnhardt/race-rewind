### Previous race

Didier Pironi won at Zandvoort, with Nelson Piquet second and Rosberg third. René Arnoux crashed out at the notorious Tarzan corner on lap 22 when his Renault's throttle stuck open; he walked away unhurt from a heavy impact with the tyre barriers. Pironi closes on Watson in the championship.

### Championship standings

Watson leads the Drivers' Championship going into Brands Hatch, with Pironi within striking distance.

### Milestones

Keke Rosberg takes the first ever pole position for a Finnish driver in Formula One.

### Strategy

Brabham arrive at Brands Hatch with a new tactical plan they have been calling "The Ploy." Inspired by Prost's comeback drive at Kyalami — where he recovered from a lap down to win on fresh tyres — the team plans to send both cars out with half-full tanks and softer, faster tyres. A mid-race pit stop will refuel the car and change tyres, giving the Brabham drivers the speed to build a large enough lead to win. Both drivers are now committed to the turbocharged BMW-powered BT50 chassis.
