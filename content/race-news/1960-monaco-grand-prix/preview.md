### Championship standings

McLaren leads the championship after Argentina. Allison is second, Moss third.

### Previous race

In Buenos Aires, McLaren won from Allison and Moss. Moss had led but retired with suspension failure. Bonnier led with twelve laps remaining when his engine failed. McLaren's reliability proved decisive.

### Between-race developments

Harry Schell, who made his final Grand Prix start at the Argentine Grand Prix, died during practice for the non-championship BRDC International Trophy at Silverstone on 13 May. Schell had raced in Formula One since the early 1950s.
