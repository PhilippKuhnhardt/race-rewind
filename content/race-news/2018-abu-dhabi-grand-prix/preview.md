### Championship standings

Both championships are decided. Hamilton leads Vettel by eighty-one points in the Drivers'; Mercedes lead Ferrari by sixty-seven in the Constructors', with Red Bull third.

### Previous race

Hamilton won in Brazil from pole. Verstappen had led from lap 40 but was spun around on lap 44 by Force India's Ocon, who was attempting to unlap himself, handing the lead back to Hamilton. Bottas finished fifth, allowing Mercedes to clinch the Constructors' Championship for a fifth consecutive season. Räikkönen finished third.

### Entrants

This is the final race for Fernando Alonso, who has announced he will not return to Formula One in 2019. McLaren runs a special one-off livery in his honour.

### Practice

Verstappen was fastest in FP1, Bottas in FP2, Hamilton in FP3.
