### Championship standings

Piastri leads the Drivers' Championship with 234 points, eight ahead of Norris and sixty-nine ahead of Verstappen in third.

### Previous race

Norris won the British Grand Prix at Silverstone in wet and changing conditions to become the thirteenth British driver to win their home race. Piastri received a ten-second penalty for erratic braking during a safety car period and dropped from the lead. Hülkenberg — starting from nineteenth and last — finished third, his maiden career podium at his 239th race start, setting a new record for the longest wait for a first podium. Sauber's last podium before Hülkenberg's had been Kamui Kobayashi's third place at the 2012 Japanese Grand Prix.

### Between-race developments

Red Bull Racing team principal Christian Horner was sacked three days after the British Grand Prix. Laurent Mekies, previously Racing Bulls team principal, takes over at Red Bull Racing from this round. Alan Permane steps up to lead Racing Bulls.

### Sprint format

Belgium is the third sprint weekend of the season.

### Tyre choices

Pirelli brings the C1, C3 and C4 compounds — designated hard, medium and soft.

### Practice

The sole free practice session was topped by Piastri ahead of Verstappen and Norris.
