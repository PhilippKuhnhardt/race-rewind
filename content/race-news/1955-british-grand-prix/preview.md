### Previous race

At Zandvoort, Fangio and Moss finished over a minute clear of Luigi Musso's Maserati despite a circuit made slippery by continuous drizzle. It was the first podium since the 1950 French Grand Prix at which no Ferrari-powered car appeared.

### Championship standings

Fangio leads the championship after back-to-back victories for Mercedes in Belgium and the Netherlands, with Moss his closest challenger.

### Between-race developments

The French Grand Prix, rescheduled from 3 July to 25 September following the Le Mans disaster, remains on the calendar.

### Entrants

Jack Brabham makes his Formula One debut at Aintree.

### Track changes

The British Grand Prix moves from Silverstone to Aintree for the first time, as part of a rotation between the two circuits. Aintree was purpose-built around the Grand National horse-racing course near Liverpool and was designed at the time of its construction to be one of the safest and most modern facilities in Europe.
