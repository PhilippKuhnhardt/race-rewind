### Championship standings

Hamilton leads Bottas by 36 points. Mercedes leads Ferrari by 140 in the Constructors'.

### Previous race

Hamilton won France by 18 seconds over Bottas, with Leclerc third less than a second behind Bottas. Vettel pitted for soft tyres on the penultimate lap in a bid to take the fastest lap bonus point, which he claimed on the final lap.

### Penalties

Sainz and Albon are required to start from the back of the grid for exceeding their power unit component quotas. Hülkenberg and Magnussen each carry five-place grid penalties — Hülkenberg for exceeding his power unit quota, Magnussen for an unscheduled gearbox change.

### Practice

Hamilton was fastest in FP1 with Vettel second. FP2 was eventful: Vettel spun, and both Bottas and Verstappen crashed. Leclerc was quickest in FP2 ahead of Bottas, Gasly, and Hamilton. Leclerc again topped FP3 ahead of Hamilton, Bottas, and Vettel.
