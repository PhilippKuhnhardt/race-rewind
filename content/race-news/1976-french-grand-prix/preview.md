### Championship standings

Lauda's lead at this point is over the Tyrrell pair of Depailler with 26 points and Scheckter with 24. Hunt sits further back. The Spanish Grand Prix result — where McLaren's appeal has yet to be heard — continues to suppress Hunt's official tally.

### Previous race

The Tyrrell P34 took its first victory at Anderstorp in Sweden, with Scheckter leading Depailler home for a six-wheel 1–2. Andretti led for much of the race in the Lotus before his engine failed on lap 46. Lauda moved into third after Chris Amon's Ensign lost suspension, with Hunt fifth. The P34's dominance of the race alarmed many of the other teams.
