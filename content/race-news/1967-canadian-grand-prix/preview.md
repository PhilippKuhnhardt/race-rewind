### Championship standings

Hulme leads with 37 points; Brabham has 25. Clark and Amon share third place on 19 points each.

### Previous race

Hulme won in Germany after Gurney's Eagle-Weslake led comfortably until a broken driveshaft on lap 13 handed him victory. Clark's early lead ended with a slowly deflating tyre; Hill recovered from a chaotic start to reach tenth before mechanical failure ended his race. Brabham finished second; Amon third.

### Between-race developments

Pedro Rodríguez crashed heavily at the non-championship Mediterranean Grand Prix, a Formula Two race at Autodromo di Pergusa, and will be absent for approximately two months. Richard Attwood and Jacky Ickx join Cooper as replacements. It is Ickx's first Formula One championship start; he is also leading the European Formula Two Championship this year.

### Circuit

The Canadian Grand Prix takes place for the first time as a World Championship round, held at Mosport Park in Bowmanville, Ontario. The event was planned as a one-off in celebration of Canada's centenary of independence.
