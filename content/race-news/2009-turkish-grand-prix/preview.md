### Championship standings

Button leads by 16 points from teammate Barrichello. Brawn lead the Constructors' Championship by 43.5 points from Red Bull.

### Previous race

Button dominated Monaco from pole, leading throughout to win ahead of Barrichello and Räikkönen — Ferrari's first podium of the season. Vettel dropped out of contention when his supersoft tyres began losing grip rapidly after 12 laps, sliding into the barriers at Sainte Dévote. Kovalainen and Nakajima crashed in the closing stages. Sébastien Bourdais scored what would prove to be his last championship points.

### Tyre choices

Bridgestone brings the hard and soft compounds.

### Practice

Williams, Ferrari, and McLaren were competitive in FP1, with Rosberg, Hamilton, and Trulli leading the times. Kovalainen, Alonso, and Kubica topped FP2. After mixed Friday sessions, only Williams was consistently quick, while Brawn and Red Bull ran mid-field in both sessions.
