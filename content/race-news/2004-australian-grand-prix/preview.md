### Entrants

Christian Klien makes his Formula One debut for Jaguar. Bas Leinders, entered as Minardi's third free practice driver, has been denied an FIA Super Licence due to insufficient mileage in a Formula One car; the team is seeking to satisfy the requirement before the next race.

### Milestones

This race is the 150th Grand Prix for the McLaren-Mercedes partnership, in place since 1995. It is also the first race since the 2001 San Marino Grand Prix that cars compete without fully-automatic gearboxes and launch control, both of which were banned by the FIA ahead of this season. Traction control remains permitted.

### Car upgrades

Ferrari arrives at Albert Park with their new F2004 — unusual in that they have not raced it elsewhere first. Renault have focused on reducing the asymmetrically-mounted radiating masses on their R24.
