The season opens on New Year's Day at East London. Originally scheduled as the final race of the 1964 season, the South African Grand Prix was moved back a week — which means every car on the grid is a 1964-specification machine, with no time to develop 1965 designs.

### Entrants

This race marks the Formula One World Championship debut of Jackie Stewart, who joins BRM alongside Graham Hill. Rhodesian constructor RE also makes its championship debut, entering a car for a local driver. A special pre-qualifying session is run for invited local drivers, who must lap in under 1 minute 37 seconds to advance to the main qualifying session.

### Practice

Jim Clark completed the first ever lap of the East London circuit at over 100 mph during practice.
