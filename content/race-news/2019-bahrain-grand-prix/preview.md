### Championship standings

Bottas leads Hamilton by 8 points after Australia. Mercedes holds a 22-point advantage over Ferrari in the Constructors'.

### Previous race

Bottas drove away from second on the grid to win Australia by more than 20 seconds over Hamilton, with Verstappen third — the first podium for a Honda-powered car since the 2008 British Grand Prix. Bottas also claimed the first fastest lap bonus point since Maurice Trintignant at the 1959 United States Grand Prix.

### Entrants

Ferrari's Mission Winnow branding returns this weekend, having been banned at the previous race in Australia for local tobacco advertising reasons.

### Track changes

An extra DRS zone has been added between turns 3 and 4, in addition to the existing zones between turns 15 and 1 and between turns 10 and 11. The change is expected to increase overtaking opportunities and promote more pit stops.

### Practice

Ferrari dominated all three practice sessions. Leclerc led Vettel in each session, with Leclerc's pace comfortably more than half a second ahead of the next-fastest constructor in FP1.
