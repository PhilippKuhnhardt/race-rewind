### Championship standings

Prost leads by one point from Piquet after Alboreto's victory in Detroit left both championship contenders off the scoresheet. Tambay and Rosberg are both in close attendance.

### Previous race

Michele Alboreto won Detroit for Tyrrell-Ford after Piquet picked up a slow rear puncture on lap 51 while leading. Arnoux had led from early on but retired with an electrical failure. Prost failed to score; Rosberg ran second and Watson third — both making late charges through the field from deep on the grid.

### Entrants

Jacques Villeneuve, brother of the late Gilles Villeneuve for whom this circuit is named, makes his Formula One debut at his home race, given a drive by RAM Racing. The race is held at the Circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montreal.
